Competitive Positioning

How the portfolio wins against each competitor category

vs. Schneider / Siemens / Hitachi

  • ✓ 10x lower price point — accessible to African utilities
  • ✓ Designed for intermittent connectivity — works on 2G/3G
  • ✓ Local field operations — mobile-first, offline-capable
  • ✓ Outcome-based pricing — aligned with utility cash flow
  • ✓ Rapid deployment — weeks not months

vs. BPS / Awesense

  • ✓ Hardware + Software integrated stack — not software-only
  • ✓ Broader geographic scalability — pan-African from day 1
  • ✓ Carbon credit monetization — additional revenue stream
  • ✓ Open platform vs. closed proprietary — API ecosystem
  • ✓ Predictive maintenance — proactive vs. reactive analytics

vs. Mini-grid Operators

  • ✓ Grid-to-grid integration — connects national + mini + off-grid
  • ✓ Enterprise-grade analytics — not siloed operations
  • ✓ Utility partnership model — leverages existing infrastructure
  • ✓ Scalable software economics — 80%+ gross margins

vs. Traditional Hardware Vendors

  • ✓ AI-native design — not bolt-on analytics
  • ✓ Low-bandwidth optimization — works in connectivity-constrained environments
  • ✓ SaaS business model — OPEX not CAPEX
  • ✓ Rapid deployment — no 18-month implementation cycles

# Privacy Policy

## Page Title & Description
Public and authenticated policy page explaining how GridIQ handles personal data, customer records, utility data, telemetry, audit logs, and regulator-facing submissions.

## User Roles & Access Control
- Public guests can view.
- Authenticated users can view workspace-specific policy version where applicable.
- Administrators can manage policy versions elsewhere, not on this public page.

## UI/UX Component Breakdown
- Policy content sections.
- Effective date and version.
- Data categories summary.
- Contact information.
- Download PDF button.
- Workspace-specific addendum link for authenticated users.

## User Actions & Interactions
- Read policy.
- Download PDF.
- Open terms of service.
- Contact privacy officer.
- View data-governance addendum if authorised.

## Data Requirements & State
- Fetches current published policy, effective date, jurisdictional addenda, and contact details.
- Captures no sensitive form data unless contact form is enabled.
- Logs policy publication separately in administration workflow.

## Navigation & Routing
- `/privacy` links to `/terms`, `/data-governance` for authorised users, and `/login`.
- Missing policy version shows latest active approved version.

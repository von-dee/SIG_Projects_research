Here's a breakdown of the most widely used software in the **mineral/hard rock mining** space, organized by function:

---

## 🪨 Geological Modeling & Resource Estimation

- **Surpac (Dassault Systèmes)** — one of the most widely used mining software platforms globally, supporting geological modeling, mine planning, and resource management for large-scale operations. It's the de facto industry standard at many majors.

- **Leapfrog (Seequent)** — a powerful 3D geological modeling tool used for resource estimation, exploration, and mine planning, known for real-time data analysis and visualization that helps reduce risk and improve project outcomes.

- **Datamine** — a leader in integrated mining software, providing robust tools for mine design, resource estimation, and production scheduling across the entire mine life cycle.

---

## 🗺️ Mine Design & Planning

- **Vulcan (Maptek)** — a highly effective 3D mine design and resource modeling software, ideal for both open-pit and underground mining operations.

- **Deswik** — a digital solution that automates numerous tasks through multiple modules, enabling mine engineers and managers to operate more efficiently. Widely used for scheduling.

- **MineSight (Hexagon)** — a suite of tools for mine planning, scheduling, and production optimization, widely recognized for its robust 3D design and real-time tracking capabilities.

---

## 🏗️ Operations & Fleet Management

- **Hexagon Mining (formerly Jigsaw/Leica)** — offers a full suite of mining software solutions designed to improve safety and efficiency, including fleet management and collision avoidance systems.

- **Maptek I-Site** — a powerful software solution for laser scanning and 3D modeling, commonly used for surveying and mine design.

---

## ⚙️ Geotechnical & Safety

- **Rocscience** — an industry-leading developer of geotechnical software specializing in 2D and 3D modeling and analysis of rock and soil, used widely across civil and mining applications to help improve safety and reduce costs.

- **SHEQSY** — rated as a leading lone worker safety software, enabling miners to accomplish tasks with confidence whether in open pit or underground sites.

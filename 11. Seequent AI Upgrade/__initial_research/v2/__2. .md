## 🏢 Tier 1: Major Mining Companies — Internal AI Platforms

These are the most advanced but **not commercially available** — they build proprietary ecosystems.

### **BHP** — Enterprise AI Ecosystem (Most Mature)
BHP has evolved from discrete pilots to a globally integrated AI hub. In May 2025, they launched the **Industry AI Hub in Singapore** as a nerve center for scaling solutions worldwide. Their proprietary machine learning models identified a **1.3 billion tonne copper-gold deposit** near Olympic Dam — the ultimate validation that AI has moved from R&D to commercial discovery. 

Key technologies deployed:
- **Digital twins** with AI models for ore characteristic prediction (reduced SAG mill losses by ~70% at Escondida)
- **Muon tomography** for subsurface scanning
- **Quantum sensing** (invested in Atomionics, September 2025)
- **Computer vision** on conveyors (reduced 1,000+ hours of downtime at WAIO)
- **Enterprise-wide AI Transformation Program** with Microsoft partnership ($18.9M revenue uplift at Escondida)

### **Rio Tinto** — RTVis + Fleet Space AI
Rio Tinto built **RTVis™** (Rio Tinto Visualisation) with a 3D gaming engine — 1,700 users across 98% of mines. They partnered with **Fleet Space Technologies** for AI-powered geophysical imaging that creates 3D subsurface maps without drilling, identifying lithium drill targets **100x faster** than traditional methods. 

Key technologies:
- **3D visualization engine** for geology, geotechnical, drill & blast, production
- **AI geophysical imaging** (sensor networks + pattern recognition)
- **Digital twins** for whole-mine optimization
- **Real-time tracking** of 4,000+ vehicles with 30M geo-positions daily

## 🖥️ Tier 2: Commercial Geological Modeling Software with AI Integration

These are the legacy tools evolving toward AI — but still limited in scope.

### **Seequent Leapfrog 2025.1** — Structural AI
The June 2025 release introduces "generational change" through integration with the **Seequent Evo platform** and applications **BlockSync** and **Driver**. Key AI-adjacent features: 

- **Refreshed structural trends** — faster modeling with fewer manual edits, new clustering settings, editable visual representations
- **Streamlined model updates** with face mapping using polylines
- **Data preparation improvements** — standardized legend coloring, streamlined mixed data source combining
- **Cross-section communication** — orthogonal vs. illustrative projection for technical vs. management audiences

**Gap:** Leapfrog has **no true AI** — it's algorithmic automation of implicit modeling, not machine learning. The "AI" is in workflow speed, not predictive capability.

### **Maptek Vulcan + DomainMCF + GeologyCore 2025** — ML Domain Generation
Maptek is the most AI-forward of the legacy modeling vendors. **DomainMCF** uses machine learning to generate domain boundaries directly from drillhole data, with models available in minutes comparable to classical techniques. **GeologyCore 2025** introduces a global **Radial Basis Function (RBF) engine** for smoother, geologically consistent surfaces. 

Key AI features:
- **ML domain boundary generation** from drillhole samples
- **Cloud compute framework** (Maptek Compute Framework)
- **Augmented reality integration**
- **Sandbox environment** for alternate interpretation testing
- **Tight integration** between GeologyCore structural trends and DomainMCF modeling

**Real-world validation:** Havilah Resources used DomainMCF at Mutooroo copper-cobalt — the AI model predicted extensions, and the first four drillholes successfully intersected mineralization close to AI-predicted depths. 

### **Datamine Studio** — Traditional Geostatistics
Still the industry standard for resource estimation and 3D block modeling, but with **limited native AI**. Relies on third-party integration for machine learning capabilities.

## 🎯 Tier 3: Specialized AI Exploration & Targeting Platforms

These focus on **exploration targeting** rather than full 3D geological modeling.

### **VRIFY AI** — Mineral Targeting Software
VRIFY uses **deep learning + computer vision** to train predictive models on drillholes, rock geochemistry, and mineral occurrences. It generates **probability maps** of mineralized areas with uncertainty factors. 

**Kodiak Copper case study:** VRIFY AI analyzed MPD project data, confirmed known mineralization, confirmed existing targets, and generated **new prospective areas**. The system uses:
- Feature engineering (vertical derivatives, tilt angle, Fourier filtering)
- Random forest regression for geochemical interpolation
- Probabilistic lithological mapping from outcrops
- Stochastic modeling with uncertainty quantification

### **KoBold Metals** — AI-Powered Exploration
Backed by BHP partnership (2021) for battery mineral discovery in Western Australia. Uses **big data analytics** and proprietary algorithms for geological pattern recognition and deposit prediction. Not a software platform — more of an AI exploration service company.

### **SensOre** — AI Drilling Target ID
BHP invested in March 2022. Focuses on **data automation** for exploration optimization — reportedly contributed to unlocking **$1 billion in value** from data automation. 

### **Ideon REVEAL** — Subsurface Intelligence
Solution-as-a-service with **proprietary hardware + software**. Delivers high-resolution 3D/4D subsurface models with quantified uncertainty. Uses **muon tomography** + AI interpretation. Focuses on **mining value chain transformation** — from exploration through closure.

## 🔬 Tier 5: Core Scanning & Field AI Technologies

These handle **data ingestion** and **multimodal sensing** but not integrated modeling.

### **GeologicAI** — Multi-Sensor Core Scanning
Mobile trailer units with **five sensors**: hyperspectral (SWIR/VNIR), XRF, high-res RGB, LiDAR, magnetic susceptibility. Robotic system scans 300-500m/day. Added **LIBS** (Laser-Induced Breakdown Spectroscopy) in December 2025 for rare earth elements. Pixel-level AI fusion generates mineral maps, elemental curves, automated RQD, sulfide identification, vein detection. **BHP invested $44M Series B in July 2025.** 

### **Datarock** — AI Core Logging
Automated rock typing, structure & mineralogy identification from core images. Computer vision pipeline for speed and consistency.

### **Imago** — Geological Image Management
Cloud-based repository with collaborative annotation and AI-enhanced feature identification. Team integration across locations.

### **Veracio TruScan** — Hyperspectral Core Hardware
Competitor to GeologicAI. Minalyzer CS system, field-deployable hyperspectral analysis.

## 🌊 Tier 6: Seismic & Subsurface AI Interpretation

These focus on **seismic interpretation** — closer to oil & gas than mining geology.

### **Eliis PaleoScan** — Seismic Interpretation Platform
Global standard combining **Relative Geological Time (RGT) technology** with **AI-assisted tools**. Patented RGT transforms seismic interpretation with unprecedented time savings. Energy transition focus. 

### **subsurfaceAI** — AI-Infused Seismic Interpretation
**20-200x faster** than legacy software. 2D/3D seismic + well data integration. Core photo interpretation for litho-facies, Vshale, porosity, permeability. 4D visualization and SRV estimation. ML workflows for production data integration and sweet spot prediction. 

### **Ivanhoe Electric / CGI** — Deep Sulphide Detection
**Typhoon™ transmitter** generates pure, stable signals for extremely high SNR. Machine learning inversion (**CGI software**) detects sulphide minerals at **>1.5km depth**. BHP alliance partner.

Open-sourced on Hugging Face, trained on Harmonized Landsat Sentinel-2 data. **15% improvement over state-of-the-art** with half the labeled data. Commercial version through **IBM watsonx Environmental Intelligence Suite**.

## 🎯 The Critical Gap

**No commercially available platform integrates all seven technologies into a single geological modeling workflow.**

- **BHP and Rio Tinto** build internal ecosystems — but these are proprietary, not for sale
- **Leapfrog/Maptek** focus on 3D modeling with limited AI (structural automation, ML domains)
- **VRIFY/KoBold** focus on exploration targeting, not full 3D subsurface modeling
- **subsurfaceAI** focuses on seismic interpretation (oil & gas bias)
- **Farmonaut/LYRASENSE** focus on satellite imagery, not drill log integration
- **GeologicAI** focuses on core scanning, not 3D model generation
- **IBM/NASA Geo FM** is satellite-only, not subsurface

**The convergence opportunity remains wide open:** A single platform that fuses foundation models (GeoGPT), physics-informed neural networks, diffusion-based subsurface generation, graph neural networks for spatial relationships, multimodal LLMs for natural language queries, semi-supervised transfer learning for sparse data, and explainable AI for regulatory compliance — all within a geological modeling workflow that exports to Surpac/Leapfrog/Vulcan.

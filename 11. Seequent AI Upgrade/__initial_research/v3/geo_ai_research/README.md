## The Seven Technologies

1. **Foundation Models for Geoscience** — Domain-specific LLMs (GeoGPT) trained on geoscience literature
2. **Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs)** — Embedding physical laws into neural networks for real-time simulation
3. **Generative AI & Diffusion Models** — Probabilistic subsurface generation with physics constraints (KADM)
4. **Graph Neural Networks (GNNs)** — Modeling spatial relationships between geological features (KDCGAT, ADGCN)
5. **Multimodal LLMs for Earth Observation** — Natural language queries across satellite and subsurface data (MineAgent)
6. **Semi-Supervised & Transfer Learning** — Learning from sparse labeled data and transferring across deposits
7. **Explainable AI (XAI)** — Transparent predictions for regulatory compliance (SHAP, attention maps)

## Key Finding

**No existing software platform integrates all seven technologies.** The convergence opportunity is wide open — a single platform that fuses these capabilities into a unified geological modeling workflow with natural language interface and regulatory compliance does not exist yet.

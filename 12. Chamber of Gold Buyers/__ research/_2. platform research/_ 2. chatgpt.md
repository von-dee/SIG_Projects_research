
If you're looking at **B2B goods sales platforms** (marketplaces where businesses buy and sell products), their business models generally revolve around commissions, subscriptions, financing, logistics, and value-added services.

Here are some of the leading B2B sales platforms and how they make money:

| Platform        | Industry                  | Core Business Model                                             |
| --------------- | ------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Alibaba.com     | Global wholesale          | Supplier memberships, advertising, trade assurance, commissions |
| Amazon Business | Multi-industry            | Product margins, subscriptions, advertising, fulfillment        |
| IndiaMART       | Manufacturing & wholesale | Supplier subscriptions, premium listings, lead generation       |
| Faire           | Retail wholesale          | Commission on orders, payment processing, financing             |
| Udaan           | FMCG & retail             | Transaction fees, logistics, credit financing                   |
| Mercateo        | Procurement               | Procurement SaaS, transaction fees                              |
| Thomasnet       | Industrial                | Supplier advertising, premium listings                          |
| Global Sources  | International trade       | Supplier subscriptions, exhibitions, advertising                |

### Typical business models

1. **Commission-based Marketplace**

* Charge 1–15% per transaction.
* Best for high transaction volumes.

Examples:

* Alibaba
* Faire

---

2. **Subscription (SaaS)**

Suppliers pay monthly or yearly for:

* Online storefront
* More product listings
* Analytics
* CRM
* Priority search ranking

Example pricing:

* Free
* Standard – $49/month
* Professional – $199/month
* Enterprise – Custom

---

3. **Lead Generation**

Instead of charging per sale:

* Buyers submit RFQs.
* Suppliers pay to receive verified leads.

Examples:

* IndiaMART
* Thomasnet

---

4. **Advertising**

Revenue from:

* Sponsored products
* Homepage banners
* Search result promotion
* Featured suppliers

---

5. **Logistics Revenue**

Platform earns from:

* Shipping
* Warehousing
* Customs clearance
* Delivery

Examples:

* Amazon Business
* Udaan

---

6. **Trade Financing**

Provide:

* Buy Now Pay Later
* Working capital loans
* Invoice financing
* Supply chain financing

This often becomes one of the largest revenue sources as the platform scales.

---

7. **Payment Processing**

Charge transaction fees on:

* Credit cards
* Bank transfers
* Escrow services
* Multi-currency payments

---

8. **Procurement Software (SaaS)**

Sell procurement software to organizations for:

* Purchase requests
* Purchase orders
* Approval workflows
* Budget control
* Vendor management

Customers pay annual licenses or subscriptions.

---

9. **Value-added Services**

Additional revenue can come from:

* Product inspections
* Quality certification
* Insurance
* Supplier verification
* Analytics
* Market intelligence
* ESG reporting

### Opportunity in Ghana

For sectors such as mining, agriculture, construction, and manufacturing, a Ghana-focused B2B marketplace could serve suppliers of equipment, spare parts, consumables, and services. Beyond transaction commissions, the platform could monetize through supplier subscriptions, RFQ lead generation, logistics coordination, escrow payments, trade finance partnerships, and procurement software for large organizations.

Given your background in mining technology and government digital platforms, a vertical B2B marketplace focused on mining and industrial procurement could differentiate itself with features such as equipment traceability, supplier verification, regulatory compliance, contract management, and integration with enterprise procurement systems.

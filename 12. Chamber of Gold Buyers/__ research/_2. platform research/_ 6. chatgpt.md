## For a Gold Buyers Chamber

```text
Gold Seller

      ▼

      │
      ▼
RFQ / Bid / Offer

      ▼
Trade Confirmation
      │
      ▼

      │

```

## A stronger vision

Based on your previous work in the Ghana mining sector, I would go beyond a marketplace and position it as a **Digital Gold Trade Ecosystem**. The marketplace becomes one module within a broader platform that also supports compliance, member management, document workflows, market intelligence, logistics, financing, and reporting.

This gives the Chamber value even outside of active trading and creates multiple revenue opportunities for your company.

The closest real-world inspirations for such a platform are:

1. Metalshub (best for RFQ-based metals trading)

3. Alibaba.com (best for marketplace concepts)
4. IndiaMART (best for membership and lead-generation models)

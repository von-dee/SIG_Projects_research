## SXSW GO Mobile App — Overview

SXSW GO is the official mobile application for the South by Southwest (SXSW) Conference & Festivals, serving as the primary digital companion for attendees across all three tracks: **Innovation**, **Film & TV**, and **Music**. The app is free to download on iOS and Android and functions as the central hub for scheduling, networking, reservations, and real-time event navigation during the festival (March 12–18, 2026).

## Site Map & App Structure

| Section | Description |

## Key Pages & Features (Detailed)

### **1. Authentication & Onboarding**
- **Login Page**: Sign in via SXSW ID (`id.sxsw.com`) or create a new account.
- **Badge Linking Flow**: Enter SXSW Link Code (found in registration confirmation email or purchase history) to link credential to the app. This unlocks premium features like reservations and networking.

### **2. Schedule & Discovery**
- **Full Schedule Browser**: Conference sessions, film screenings, music showcases, comedy shows, exhibitions, and special events.
- **Event Detail Pages**: Speaker/artist bios, trailers, audio previews, venue info, capacity status.
- **Favorites System**: Star events to add to "My Schedule"; syncs across mobile and web.
- **Event Status Indicators**: Real-time capacity updates (e.g., "Seats Available," "Limited," "Full") visible in-app and on the web schedule.

### **3. Reservations System**
- **Daily Reservation Limits**:
  - **Platinum Badge**: 3 reservations per day
  - **Innovation, Film & TV, Music Badges**: 2 reservations per day
- **Reservation Windows**: Staggered release dates (e.g., Platinum opens first on Feb 19; other badges on Feb 26; bonus windows on March 5).
- **Mentor Sessions & Workshops**: Require separate reservations; do not count against daily limits.
- **Waitlist Functionality**: Join waitlists for fully booked events; automatic email confirmation if a spot opens.

### **4. Networking & Social**
- **Attendee Directory**: Search by name, company, role, or interest.
- **Direct Messaging**: Send messages to other attendees who have opted into the attendee list.
- **Profile Visibility Controls**: Adjust who can see your profile and contact you.
- **SXSW Recommends (People)**: ML-driven suggestions for attendees to connect with based on profile data and schedule overlap.

### **5. Location & Contextual Services**
- **Events Nearby**: Uses GPS + SXSW Schedule Event Status to show real-time nearby happenings.
- **Bluetooth Beacons**: Enables personalized recommendations and proximity-based networking when enabled.
- **Push Notifications**: Real-time alerts for event changes, reservation windows, and personalized recommendations.

### **6. Support & Help**
- **Abby, the SXSW Chatbot** (historically included): Conversational AI for FAQs and navigation help.
- **Tech Support**: Email `techsupport@sxsw.com` for badge linking or app issues.

## Business Model & How SXSW GO Makes Money

The SXSW GO app itself is **not a direct revenue generator** in the traditional sense (it is free to download with no in-app purchases, ads, or subscriptions). Instead, it operates as a **strategic enabler** of SXSW’s broader business model, driving revenue indirectly through the following mechanisms:

### **1. Badge & Registration Sales (Primary Revenue Driver)**
- The app is functionally useless without a linked SXSW badge, creating a hard dependency that drives badge purchases.
- Badge prices for 2026:
  - **Platinum**: $1,995 ($2,095 walk-up)
  - **Innovation**: $1,395 ($1,495 walk-up)
  - **Film & TV** and **Music**: Similar tiered pricing
- The reservation system is a **premium feature** gated behind badge linking, incentivizing higher-tier badge purchases (Platinum gets more daily reservations).

### **2. Sponsorship & Brand Partnerships**
- The app serves as a **high-value sponsorship asset**:
  - **Sponsored Recommendations**: Brands can pay to have their sessions, showcases, or activations featured in the "SXSW Recommends" algorithm.
  - **In-App Brand Presence**: Sponsored push notifications, branded event listings, and featured placements.
  - **Beacon/Location Marketing**: Bluetooth and location services enable sponsors to deliver proximity-based promotions and activations.
- Sponsorships account for approximately **20% of SXSW’s total revenue**.

### **3. Data Monetization & Audience Intelligence**
- The app collects rich behavioral data:
  - Session attendance and reservation patterns
  - Networking activity and messaging
  - Location movement and dwell time
  - Schedule preferences and interests
- This data is invaluable for:
  - **Sponsor ROI reporting**: Demonstrating attendee engagement with brand activations.
  - **Future programming decisions**: Optimizing track offerings and venue allocation.
  - **Targeted marketing**: Re-engaging attendees for future SXSW events and sister properties (SXSW EDU, SXSW Sydney).

### **4. Expo & Exhibitor Value-Add**
- The app drives foot traffic to the **SXSW Expo** (moved to Fairmont Hotel in 2026), where companies pay exhibitor fees to showcase products.
- Real-time event status and nearby features direct attendees to expo floors, increasing exhibitor satisfaction and justifying high booth costs.

### **5. SXSW EDU & Ecosystem Expansion**
- SXSW operates a parallel app, **SXSW EDU GO**, for its education conference, using the same codebase and infrastructure. This extends the platform’s utility across SXSW’s year-round portfolio without significant additional development cost.

### **6. Indirect Revenue: Housing & Travel**
- The app integrates with SXSW’s official housing and travel partners, driving bookings through affiliate or partnership arrangements.

## Summary: The SXSW GO Value Proposition

| Aspect | Detail |
|---|---|
| **App Cost to User** | Free (iOS & Android) |
| **Direct Revenue from App** | $0 — no ads, no IAP, no subscriptions |
| **Primary Function** | Badge-linked utility tool for scheduling, reservations, and networking |
| **Business Model** | **Freemium ecosystem enabler** — the app drives badge sales, sponsorships, and data value |
| **Key Monetization Levers** | Badge tier upsells, sponsored placements, exhibitor foot traffic, attendee data intelligence |
| **Strategic Role** | Critical infrastructure for SXSW’s ~48% revenue from registrations and ~20% from sponsorships |

In essence, SXSW GO is a **loss-leading platform asset** designed to maximize the value of SXSW’s $1,995 Platinum badges and $500K+ sponsorship packages by ensuring attendees have a seamless, data-rich, and socially connected experience that justifies the high cost of attendance and keeps them returning year after year.

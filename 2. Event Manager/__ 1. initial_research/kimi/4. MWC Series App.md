Based on the official GSMA documentation and privacy notices, here is a comprehensive breakdown of the **MWC Series App** (ID 960712096), its structure, functionality, and business model.

---

## App Overview

The **MWC Series App** is the official mobile application for the Mobile World Congress (MWC) series of events produced by the **GSMA** (Groupe Speciale Mobile Association). It serves as the primary digital companion for attendees across all MWC events (Barcelona, Shanghai, Kigali, Doha, etc.) and the M360 events series. The app is **mandatory for all attendees** to download for event access.

## Core Features & Functionality

### **1. Digital Access & Entry Management**
- The app generates a **Digital Access Pass** (QR code) that is required for entry into MWC events and specific zones
- Streamlines on-site registration and badge pickup

### **2. Agenda & Session Management**
- Browse the full event programme
- Build personal schedules with reminders
- Receive recommendations based on profile and interests

### **3. Interactive Venue Mapping**
- Navigate complex exhibition halls
- Locate exhibitor stands and session rooms
- Receive recommendations based on proximity and interests

### **4. Networking & Community**
- **9-month networking window** — Attendees can connect across the entire MWC and M360 events series, extending value well beyond a single event
- Opt-in professional networking with direct messaging
- LinkedIn integration for profile enrichment and content sharing
- Favourites and contact management (up to 500 favourites/day limit)

### **5. Exhibitor Discovery & Engagement**
- Search and filter exhibitors by category, sector, and interests
- Request meetings via email
- Download product literature and assets
- Receive personalized exhibitor recommendations

### **6. Personalized Recommendations**
- AI-driven suggestions for sessions, speakers, exhibitors, and networking contacts
- Based on profile data, stated interests, event behavior, and mobile analytics (with consent)

### **7. Content & Media**
- Event news and #MWC updates
- Sponsor content and branded experiences
- Downloadable resources (whitepapers, case studies, presentations)

## Business Model & Revenue Streams

The MWC Series App operates on a **B2B event SaaS / platform monetization model** rather than a consumer app model. Revenue is generated primarily from **exhibitors, sponsors, and event partners**, not from end-user downloads or in-app purchases.

### **Revenue Stream 1: Tiered Exhibitor Digital Profiles (Freemium SaaS)**

All exhibitors receive a **Standard Profile** at no additional cost, but GSMA aggressively upsells enhanced digital visibility through tiered packages:

| Tier | Price | Key Features |
|------|-------|------------|
| **Standard** | Included with booth | 100-word description, stand location, contact info, basic meeting requests |
| **Level 1: Enhanced Profile** | £2,550 | Company logo, 4 product showcases, 6 downloadable assets, social media links |
| **Level 2: Promoted Exhibitor** | £8,150 | Rotating logo on app & website homepages, direct website links, ROI reporting |
| **Level 3: Personalised Content** | £26,500 | Top slot positioning, priority logo placement, sidebar menu placement, 20 assets, custom content programme, custom branding |
| **App Headline Sponsor** | £61,500 | Fixed position on app home page, daily messaging changes, all Level 1-3 features |

**Key insight:** 19 of the top 20 most visited exhibitor profiles purchased an enhanced digital product, demonstrating strong conversion and ROI justification.

### **Revenue Stream 2: Targeted Messaging & Lead Generation**

- **Exhibitor Targeted Messaging**: £9,495 for up to 1,000 personalized messages
- Allows exhibitors to send direct, branded communications to opted-in attendees via the app's inbox
- Custom demographic targeting using attendee data
- Generates qualified leads pre-event, during, and post-event

### **Revenue Stream 3: Sponsorship & Advertising**

- **App Headline Sponsorship**: Fixed branding on the app home page (£61,500)
- **Rotating Logos**: Premium placement on home screens and key navigation points
- **Native Content Integration**: Sponsored content programmes, branded challenges, and curated experiences
- **Banner/Native Ad Placements**: Contextual advertising within the app experience (though less intrusive than typical consumer apps)

### **Revenue Stream 4: Event Registration & Ticket Sales (Indirect)**

- While the app itself is free, it is the **mandatory gateway** for accessing the event
- The app drives ticket sales and premium pass upgrades by creating FOMO through agenda previews, networking visibility, and exclusive content
- Attendees must register for the event to unlock full app functionality

### **Revenue Stream 5: Data & Analytics Services**

- Post-event ROI reporting for exhibitors (included in Level 2+ packages)
- Aggregated behavioral analytics on attendee engagement, session popularity, and exhibitor traffic
- These insights help exhibitors justify spend and become a recurring revenue driver for GSMA

### **Revenue Stream 6: Networking Platform Extension**

- The 9-month cross-event networking capability creates a **subscription-like retention model**
- Keeps the GSMA ecosystem active year-round, increasing the likelihood of repeat event attendance and ongoing sponsor engagement

## How Money Is Made: The Economics

| Mechanism | Who Pays | Value Exchange |
|-----------|----------|----------------|
| **Enhanced Digital Profiles** | Exhibitors | Increased visibility, lead generation, brand awareness |
| **Targeted Messaging** | Exhibitors/Sponsors | Direct access to qualified prospects, meeting bookings |
| **App Sponsorships** | Major brands | Massive exposure to 100K+ high-value attendees |
| **Event Tickets** | Attendees | Physical access + digital networking + content |
| **Data/Analytics** | Exhibitors | Performance metrics, ROI justification for future spend |

### **Why This Model Works**

1. **Captive Audience**: The app is mandatory for event access, ensuring near-100% adoption among attendees
2. **High-Value Demographic**: MWC attendees are senior decision-makers in telecom, tech, and enterprise — extremely valuable for B2B advertisers
3. **Network Effects**: More attendees = more valuable networking = more exhibitors = more sponsor demand
4. **Year-Round Engagement**: The 9-month networking window transforms a 4-day event app into a continuous community platform
5. **Freemium Upsell**: Standard profiles are free, but the data proves enhanced profiles drive exponentially more traffic, creating natural upgrade pressure

---

## Summary

The MWC Series App is not monetized through consumer-facing tactics like ads or in-app purchases. Instead, it functions as a **B2B event operating system** where GSMA monetizes exhibitor desire for visibility, lead generation, and brand positioning. The app itself is a loss-leading platform that drives the core economics of the MWC events business: **exhibitor fees, sponsorships, and ticket sales**. The digital products layered on top (enhanced profiles, messaging, sponsorships) represent high-margin, scalable software revenue that extends the value of the physical event into a year-round digital ecosystem.

# Future Minerals Forum (FMF26) - App Site Map & Features

Based on an analysis of the Future Minerals Forum (FMF) event ecosystem and the mobile app listings (iOS/Android), the app serves as a comprehensive event management and networking tool for one of the world's most critical mineral sector summits. It caters to high-level government officials, mining executives, investors, and technologists.

Below is a detailed site map and feature breakdown for the **FMF26 App**.

## 2. Detailed App Features

### A. Onboarding & Access Control
*   **Secure Authentication:** Email/password login, with specific invitation code support (due to the VIP/high-level nature of the event).
*   **Dynamic Registration:** Allows users to complete their registration or purchase passes directly through the app if they are not yet registered.
*   **Profile Creation Wizard:** Step-by-step process to upload a photo, add job titles, select primary interests (e.g., Critical Minerals, ESG, Tech Integration), and set networking availability.
*   **Privacy Controls:** Granular settings allowing users to choose who can message them (Everyone, My Matches, No One) and what profile details are publicly visible.

### B. Agenda & Session Management
*   **Real-Time Agenda:** Continuously updated schedule reflecting any last-minute room changes or speaker substitutions.
*   **Smart Filtering:** Filter sessions by thematic tracks (e.g., Macro-Economic Trends, Supply Chain Resilience, Innovation in Extraction, Saudi Vision 2030).
*   **Personal Itinerary:** "Add to My Schedule" functionality with automated reminders 15 minutes before a session starts.
*   **Interactive Q&A:** In-session feature allowing attendees to upvote questions and submit their own to moderators.
*   **Live Polling:** Real-time audience polling during keynotes and panels.

### C. AI-Powered Networking & Matchmaking
*   **Smart Recommendations:** Algorithm suggests attendees, speakers, and exhibitors based on shared interests, mutual goals, or overlapping session attendance.
*   **1-on-1 Meeting Scheduler:** Users can propose meetings at specific times, selecting from available time slots in the recipient's calendar. Integrated with the venue map to suggest meeting pods/cafes.
*   **In-App Messaging:** Secure, read-receipt-enabled chat functionality with the ability to share vCards (virtual business cards) or documents.
*   **Business Card Scanning (OCR):** Ability to scan physical badges or business cards at the event to digitize contacts instantly.

### D. Exhibition & Sponsor Engagement
*   **Interactive Indoor Mapping:** GPS or beacon-enabled indoor navigation to help attendees find specific booths, restrooms, and exits.
*   **Exhibitor Content Hubs:** Digital brochures, sustainability reports, and video reels embedded within exhibitor profiles.
*   **Lead Retrieval Dashboard (Exhibitor Mode):** A special permission-level unlock for booth staff allowing them to scan attendee QR codes, add notes, and categorize leads (Hot/Warm/Cold), exporting them directly to CRM systems.

### E. Content & Knowledge Hub
*   **Resource Library:** Centralized repository for FMF26-specific reports, country mining regulations, and speaker presentations (available pre- and post-event).
*   **Real-Time Newsfeed:** A social-media-style feed curated by FMF organizers posting event highlights, photo galleries, and breaking announcements.
*   **Multi-Lingual Support:** Full localization in English and Arabic, reflecting the host country's language.

### F. Logistics & Location Services
*   **Venue Wayfinding:** Detailed maps of the King Abdulaziz International Conference Center (KAICC) and surrounding areas.
*   **Shuttle Tracking:** Live updates on official FMF transportation schedules between partner hotels and the venue.
*   **Offline Mode:** Core features (personal agenda, venue maps, saved contacts) remain accessible even with poor Wi-Fi connectivity in crowded conference halls.

### G. Engagement & Gamification
*   **QR Code Check-In:** Fast-track entry into the venue and individual breakout sessions using a dynamic, rotating QR code.
*   **Gamification/Scavenger Hunts:** Incentivizes attendees to visit different exhibition zones or attend specific sessions by scanning codes to earn points, redeemable for FMF merchandise.
*   **Push Notifications:** Deep-linked alerts for session starts, meeting reminders, and critical safety/security updates.

## 3. Technical & Platform Specifics

*   **Cross-Platform Sync:** Data syncs seamlessly between the iOS app, Android app, and the web-based attendee portal.
*   **Accessibility:** VoiceOver (iOS) and TalkBack (Android) compatibility; high-contrast mode; scalable typography.
*   **Security:** End-to-end encryption for messaging; GDPR and local Saudi data protection law (PDPL) compliance; mandatory data deletion options post-event.
*   **Backend Integration:** Hooks into the event’s primary registration system (e.g., Cvent, B2Match, or Swapcard) to ensure attendee lists and badge statuses are flawlessly synchronized.

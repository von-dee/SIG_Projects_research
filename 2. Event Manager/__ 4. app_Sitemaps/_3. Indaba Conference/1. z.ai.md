## 2. Granular Feature Specifications

### 2.1. User Profile & Identity Management
*   **Profile View Screen:** Displays the user's professional identity to other attendees.
    *   **Visual Identity:** Profile picture, First Name, Last Name, Country Flag (e.g., Ghana).
    *   **Professional Data:** Headline (e.g., "Software Engineer at Smart Innovation Ghana Limited"), Most Recent Job Title, Most Recent Company.
    *   **Privacy Controls:** "Contact details" field explicitly marked as "Private".
*   **Profile Editing:** 
    *   "Edit your Profile" link available from Home and Profile screens.
    *   **Edit Restrictions:** The app displays an "Editing Limitations" prompt, warning users that some fields may be locked or require logging into a web portal to update, ensuring data integrity with the main registration system.

### 2.2. Business Matchmaking & Networking Engine
This is the core engagement tool, designed to facilitate B2B connections.
*   **Recommendation Algorithm:** Surfaces relevant attendee profiles based on job function, company type (e.g., Mining Company Delegates vs. Software Engineers), and interests.
*   **Actionable Matching (Swipe/Select Model):**
    *   **Interested:** Add a user to the "My 'Interested' List". If mutual, a connection/chat is unlocked.
    *   **Skip:** Pass on a user, moving them to the "My 'Skip' List" to prevent them from appearing in recommendations again.
    *   **Interested in You:** A feed showing users who have already liked your profile, encouraging reciprocal action.
*   **Meeting Workflow:**
    *   **Pending Meetings:** Central hub for meeting requests awaiting approval (sent or received).
    *   **My Schedule:** Confirmed meetings appear here with time and location details (e.g., "1.51 Meeting Room").

### 2.3. Event Agenda & Itinerary Management
Provides complete visibility over the 4-day conference schedule.
*   **Master Event Agenda:**
    *   **Day Navigation:** Horizontal scrolling tabs for dates (09th to 12th).
    *   **Session Cards:** Highly structured data including Start/End Time, Session Title, Assigned Speaker/MC, and specific Stage Room (e.g., Victoria Falls Stage).
    *   **Advanced Filtering:** A "Filters" button with a numeric badge (indicating active filters) allowing users to narrow down by track (Governance, Policy, Technology), speaker, or content type.
*   **Personal Schedule (My Schedule):**
    *   **Aggregation:** Combines bookmarked conference sessions *and* confirmed matchmaking meetings into one unified timeline.
    *   **Empty State Handling:** Friendly UI with an icon and explicit text: *"You schedule is empty. You don't have any meetings or sessions scheduled"*.
    *   **Calendar Integration:** "Sync to my calendar" button to export the personal schedule to iOS Calendar, Google Calendar, or Outlook.

### 2.4. Interactive Floorplan & Venue Navigation
Digital wayfinding for the CTICC (Cape Town International Convention Centre).
*   **Multi-Level Mapping:** Floor selector (Levels 1, 2, 3) to navigate different tiers of the venue.
*   **Map UI Elements:**
    *   **Search Bar:** Find specific rooms, exhibitor booths, or stages.
    *   **Zoom & Fullscreen:** Standard pinch-to-zoom and expand controls.
    *   **Contextual Details:** Meeting rooms (e.g., "1.51 Meeting Room"), major zones (e.g., "African Mining" area), and surrounding street grids (Duncan Road, Walter Sisulu Ave) for outdoor orientation.
*   **User Engagement Features:**
    *   **Visited:** Track which booths/areas the user has physically visited (likely integrated with beacon technology or manual check-ins).
    *   **Bookmarks:** Pin important locations (like a sponsor's booth) for quick retrieval.
    *   **List View Toggle:** A "List" button to transition from the visual map to a text-based directory of map points.

### 2.5. Direct Messaging (Chats)
*   **In-App Communication:** Once two users have mutually "Interested" each other or a meeting is accepted, they unlock the ability to chat.
*   **Chat Interface:** Standard messaging UI accessible via the bottom navigation bar.

### 2.6. Global Directory & Discovery Lists
Comprehensive databases accessible from both the Home screen and the More menu.
*   **Attendee List:** Full roster of event participants with search and filter capabilities.
*   **Exhibitor List & Representatives:** Directory of companies with booths, and the specific people staffing them.
*   **Speakers List:** Directory of keynotes, MCs, and panelists.
*   **Sponsor List:** Categorized list of event sponsors.
*   **Mining Company Delegates:** A highly targeted list filtering specifically for delegates from mining operations (core to the Indaba audience).
*   **Content Summary:** Aggregated overview of key themes and takeaways from the conference content.

### 2.7. App Administration, Settings & Compliance
*   **Account Management:** 
    *   Sign Out functionality.
    *   **GDPR/Privacy Compliance:** "Delete Account" option to permanently erase user data and profile from the system.
*   **Legal Documentation:** Native in-app viewers for "Privacy Policy" and "Acceptable Use Policy".
*   **Support:** "Contact Us" form/helpdesk link and "About this Solution" (credits/tech provider info).
*   **Calendar Sync Settings:** "My Calendar Sync" in the general menu to manage integration permissions with the device's native calendar.

## 1. PRICING ANCHORS — WHERE THE NUMBERS COME FROM

### 1.1 Platform licensing benchmark
The FMF26 research (`__ 1. initial_research/kimi/5. Future Minerals Forum.md`) documents that event-app SaaS providers charge:
- **$30,000–$100,000+** baseline licensing fee to white-label and use the platform for an event cycle.

We anchor our **per-event platform build at $120,000–$280,000** — above the FMF26 generic-SaaS range, because our scope is bespoke (8 modules, AI matchmaking, live translation, indoor navigation, deal rooms) rather than a templated SaaS deployment. This premium is defensible as "gold-standard tier" per the integrated spec (`__ 2. app_research/claude/4. fully integrated.md`).

### 1.2 Lead-retrieval benchmark
FMF26 research documents lead-retrieval licences sold to exhibitors at **$200–$500 per exhibitor**. We adopt this range directly.

### 1.3 Configuration-vs-rebuild cost differential
Derived from the Master Dynamic Sitemap (`__ 5. Master Sitemap/1. Master Dynamic Sitemap.md`), which establishes that modules, roles, menus, and feature flags are data-driven (configurable), not hard-coded. Therefore:
- **First event** = full build cost.
- **Subsequent events** = configuration only (~$15k–$35k), a fraction of a rebuild.

This is the basis of the ~50% portfolio cost saving on slide 14 / Section 2.3 of the business model.

## 4. REVENUE-SHARE ASSUMPTIONS

| Model | ECP share | [Company Name] share | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|
| Per-Event | 100% of sponsor revenue ECP sells | Platform fee only | Low-risk on-ramp; ECP owns commercial |
| **Portfolio** | **75–85%** | 15–25% | Aligns incentives; ECP brings sponsors/audience, we bring platform |
| Joint Venture | 40–50% | 40–50% | Shared investment, shared ownership of sector modules |

**Why 15–25% to us in the Portfolio model:** FMF26-style SaaS vendors keep 100% of the licence but don't share in sponsor upside. Our model trades a lower headline licence for participation in the scalable revenue — meaning we only win big if the platform actually drives sponsor value. This is the alignment point to emphasise with ECP.

## 5. DATA & INSIGHTS PRICING RATIONALE

| Product | Illustrative price | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Sponsor ROI report (standalone) | $10,000–$25,000 | Comparable to bespoke market-research deliverables; priced as premium analytics |
| Portfolio intelligence report | $15,000–$40,000 | Priced against industry analyst report benchmarks (e.g., Wood Mackenzie-style sector briefs) |
| Anonymised deal-flow dataset | $25,000–$60,000 / year | Annual data-licence benchmarks; energy-sector market data is high-value |

**Why data is the Year 3 multiplier:** Compounding network effects. Year 1 has thin data; Year 3 has cross-event behaviour across 6+ events and tens of thousands of delegates. This dataset has no equivalent in the market and is the strategic asset that justifies the partnership framing.

## 6. THREE-YEAR PROJECTION — LINE-ITEM DERIVATION

### Year 1 (3 events live)
- Sponsor monetisation: 3 × $250k gross × 80% ECP share = **$600,000**
- Data products: thin, Year 1 = **$50,000**
- Platform build cost (one-time): **$280,000**
- Licence + on-site (3 events × $60k): **$180,000**
- **Net to ECP: ~$190,000**

### Year 2 (5 events live)
- Sponsor monetisation: 5 × $306k × 80% = **$1,224,000**
- Data products (growing): **$180,000**
- Config (4 events × $25k): **$100,000**
- Licence + on-site (5 × $60k): **$300,000**
- **Net to ECP: ~$954,000**

### Year 3 (6+ events live)
- Sponsor monetisation: 6 × $360k × 80% = **$1,728,000**
- Data products (mature): **$400,000**
- Config (6 × ~$15k marginal): **$90,000**
- Licence + on-site (6 × $60k): **$360,000**
- **Net to ECP: ~$1,578,000**

### 3-year cumulative
- Gross sponsor + data revenue to ECP: **~$4.0M**
- Net to ECP after platform costs: **~$2.7M**

## 7. SENSITIVITY TABLES

### 7.1 Conservative case (Year 3)
- Delegate count: 3,000/event
- Sponsor monetisation: $200k/event
- ECP rev-share: 70%
- Gross revenue: 6 × $200k × 70% = $840,000
- Data products: $200,000
- Costs: $450,000
- **Net to ECP: ~$590k**

### 7.2 Base case (Year 3) — as above
- **Net to ECP: ~$1.58M**

### 7.3 Optimistic case (Year 3)
- Delegate count: 6,000/event
- Sponsor monetisation: $450k/event
- ECP rev-share: 85%
- Gross revenue: 6 × $450k × 85% = $2,295,000
- Data products: $600,000
- Costs: $450,000
- **Net to ECP: ~$2.45M**

**Interpretation:** The model is net-positive for ECP in all three scenarios by Year 3, and in the base case by Year 2. The downside is bounded; the upside scales with ECP's audience growth.

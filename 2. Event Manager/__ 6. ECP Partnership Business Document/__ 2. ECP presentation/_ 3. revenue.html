05 / Key Strategic Point
ECP's current revenue is event-bound and seasonal. The platform's biggest opportunity isn't digitizing those existing streams — it's the shift to recurring, year-round revenue via matchmaking subscriptions, data products, and licensing, which keep generating value between events and are not capped by how many people can physically attend a single summit.
Recurring
Subscriptions, success fees, and licensing that compound month over month — not per-event spikes.
Data-Driven
Every interaction becomes sellable signal — intent, demand, sector interest — feeding back into pricing and product.
Scalable
Not capped by venue capacity or 3-day windows. The platform earns whether 200 or 20,000 delegates engage.

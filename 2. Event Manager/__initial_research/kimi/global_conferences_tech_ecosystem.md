# Global Conferences: Technology Ecosystem & Business Opportunities

## 1. World Economic Forum (WEF) — Davos

**Overview:** Annual gathering of global political and business leaders in Davos, Switzerland.

### Technology Partners
| Partner | Role |
|---|---|
| **Salesforce** | CRM, stakeholder engagement, session management |
| **Microsoft** | Cloud infrastructure, collaboration tools (Teams), AI integration |
| **SAP** | Enterprise data management and analytics |
| **Accenture** | Digital strategy and systems integration |
| **Palantir** | Data intelligence and geopolitical risk modeling |

### Systems & Software Built Around WEF
- **DAVOS App** — Official mobile app for schedules, networking, and live streams
- **UpLink Platform** — Crowdsourcing platform for global innovators to submit solutions to world challenges
- **Transformation Maps** — Interactive knowledge graph visualizing global issue interdependencies
- **Global Risks Report Portal** — Annual digital publication with interactive data

### Business Ideas
- **Policy-to-Product Pipeline Tool** — SaaS that tracks WEF policy discussions and maps them to startup opportunities
- **Executive Pre-Meeting Intelligence App** — AI briefing tool for attendees, generating tailored talking points and attendee profiles
- **Impact Metrics Dashboard** — Tool for NGOs/governments to report SDG progress aligned with WEF themes
- **Fringe Event Aggregator** — Platform listing side events, dinners, and unofficial Davos gatherings for non-accredited attendees

## 2. Consumer Electronics Show (CES) — Las Vegas

**Overview:** World's largest tech trade show, organized by the Consumer Technology Association (CTA).

### Technology Partners
| Partner | Role |
|---|---|
| **Samsung** | Anchor exhibitor, smart home and display showcases |
| **LG** | Exhibition technology and display innovation |
| **Amazon (AWS)** | Cloud infrastructure powering event apps and exhibitor platforms |
| **T-Mobile / Verizon** | 5G connectivity across the convention center |
| **Cvent** | Event registration and attendee management |

### Systems & Software Built Around CES
- **CES Official App** — Floor maps, exhibitor discovery, session scheduling
- **CTA Product Registration Portal** — For qualifying and submitting products for Innovation Awards
- **CES Media Hub** — Press portal for journalists to access embargoed releases
- **Exhibitor Resource Center** — Portal for booth management, shipping logistics, AV requests

### Business Ideas
- **Product Launch Intelligence Platform** — Aggregates CES announcements and scores their market potential using AI
- **Booth ROI Calculator** — SaaS for exhibitors to track leads, meetings, and press mentions vs. booth cost
- **CES Sourcing Connector** — B2B marketplace matching buyers (retailers, distributors) with CES exhibitors post-show
- **Launch Trend Report Subscription** — Paid newsletter/API delivering categorized CES product data to analysts and VCs

## Cross-Conference Business Ideas

These opportunities apply across multiple major global conferences:

| Idea | Description |
|---|---|
| **Universal Conference CRM** | A single CRM for professionals attending 5+ conferences per year — unified contact history, follow-up tracking, and ROI per event |
| **Conference Intelligence API** | Data API aggregating speaker lineups, topics, sponsors, and exhibitors across 100+ global events for analysts and strategists |
| **Hybrid Event Platform** | White-label platform enabling any conference to run seamless in-person + virtual experiences |
| **Sponsorship Valuation Tool** | Helps brands calculate and compare the ROI of sponsoring different global conferences |
| **Conference Visa & Logistics Concierge** | Service (or SaaS) handling visa applications, hotel, and transport for international delegates |
| **Post-Conference Learning Hub** | Platform converting conference sessions into structured micro-courses with certificates |
| **Green Conference Certification Platform** | Audits and certifies conferences for sustainability; helps organizers reduce carbon footprint |

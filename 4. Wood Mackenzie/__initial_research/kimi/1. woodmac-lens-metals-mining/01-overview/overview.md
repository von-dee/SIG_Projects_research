# Wood Mackenzie Lens Metals & Mining - Overview

## Introduction

**Wood Mackenzie Lens Metals & Mining** is an end-to-end data analytics platform designed to provide robust data analytics from mine to market. It combines the analytical power of Wood Mackenzie with the transformative capabilities of a modern data analytics platform, delivering layers of insight to attract and allocate capital effectively.

## Tagline

> "Unearth robust data analytics from mine to market. Layers of insight to attract and allocate capital."

## Platform Purpose

Lens Metals & Mining is designed to:

- **Drive efficiency** across enterprise workflows through immediate decision intelligence
- **Enable collaboration** across teams by providing a single source of truth for metals and mining data
- **Support strategic decision-making** from opportunity screening to asset valuation to market analysis
- **Deliver integrated cross-commodity insights** to capture the most bankable opportunities and inform growth strategy

## Key Value Propositions

1. **Mine to Market Coverage**: Comprehensive visibility across the entire value chain from extraction through processing to end markets
2. **Capital Allocation Intelligence**: Data-driven insights to inform investment decisions and capital deployment
3. **Asset-by-Asset Precision**: Meticulously built bottom-up data integrated with multi-sector market analysis
4. **Forecast Through 2050**: Long-term supply, demand, and price forecasts extending decades into the future
5. **Cross-Commodity Integration**: Ability to analyze multiple commodities within a single interconnected platform

## Commodities Covered

The platform covers **15 key mined commodities**:

- **Base Metals**: Copper, Aluminium, Nickel, Lead, Zinc
- **Precious Metals**: Gold
- **Battery/Critical Minerals**: Lithium, Cobalt, Graphite, Rare Earths
- **Bulk Commodities**: Coal, Iron Ore, Steel
- **Steel Alloys**: Bulk Steel Alloys, Noble Steel Alloys

## Five Integrated Solutions

1. **Assets Discovery** - Discover bankable opportunities and benchmark assets
2. **Assets Valuations** - Run post-tax financial metrics and valuation models
3. **Markets Forecast** - Comprehensive market analysis with long-term outlooks
4. **Markets Scenarios** - Compare base case vs. alternative energy transition scenarios
5. **Supply Chain** - Assess material flows and location risks across the value chain

## API & Integration

Through **Lens Direct**, Wood Mackenzie's data delivery service, users can:
- Connect to extensive datasets via API
- Download data into proprietary systems and BI tools
- Integrate with Excel, Power BI, Spotfire, and Tableau
- Seamlessly incorporate Lens data into existing workflows

# Key Features of Lens Metals & Mining

## Platform Capabilities

Lens Metals & Mining provides a comprehensive set of features designed to deliver integrated cross-commodity insights:

### 1. Asset Discovery & Mapping
- **Discover planned and existing mines**, smelting and refining facilities globally
- **View infrastructure relationships** - see how each asset relates to key infrastructure within the value chain including power plants, railways, and ports
- Visualise the complete asset landscape through interactive maps and dashboards

### 2. Resource & Cost Analysis
- **Analyse resource, reserve, and production costs** at the asset level
- Access detailed cost breakdowns including OpEx, CapEx, AISC, C1, C2, and C3 costs
- Review mine-level recoveries, strip ratios, grades, mining rates and by-products

### 3. Profitability Assessment
- **Assess profitability with crucial cost metrics**:
  - Cost curves (global and regional positioning)
  - Cash costs (C1, C2, C3)
  - Margins analysis
  - Cash flow projections
  - Operating cashflows and net revenues
  - Incentive price curves
  - NPV and IRR calculations

### 4. Data Visualisation
- **Visualise data on key market drivers** through:
  - Interactive charts and graphs
  - Geographic maps showing asset distribution
  - Industry trend analysis dashboards
  - Customisable data views

### 5. Export & Integration
- Exportable cost curves and cashflow charts
- Excel plug-in for seamless data integration
- **Lens Direct API** for automated data feeds
- Integration with major BI tools (Power BI, Spotfire, Tableau)

### 6. Weekly Data Updates
- Asset-level data refreshed weekly ensuring current market intelligence
- Operational benchmarks covering productivity, electricity, energy costs
- Cost-to-concentrates, realisation costs and freight costs tracking

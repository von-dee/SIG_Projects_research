### Cobalt

Cobalt is primarily used in lithium-ion battery cathodes and superalloys for aerospace applications. The market is concentrated in the Democratic Republic of Congo, creating supply chain risks.

**Lens Metals & Mining provides:**
- Asset-level cobalt mining data
- Supply chain risk assessment
- DRC and non-DRC production tracking
- Cost curves and production benchmarks
- Demand forecasts by end-use (batteries, alloys, chemicals)

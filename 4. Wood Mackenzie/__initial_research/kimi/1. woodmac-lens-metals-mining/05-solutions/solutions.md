# Solutions - Lens Metals & Mining

Lens Metals & Mining offers **five integrated solutions** designed to address different aspects of the metals and mining value chain. Each solution provides targeted capabilities to support specific decision-making workflows.

---

## 1. Assets Discovery

### Purpose
Discover bankable opportunities, benchmark assets to identify target areas for growth, and improve competitive position.

### Key Capabilities
- **Global Asset Database**: Access detailed data on 4,000+ mines, 750+ smelters, and 450+ metal refineries
- **Benchmarking Tools**: Compare asset performance against peers using cost curves, productivity metrics, and financial indicators
- **Opportunity Screening**: Filter and screen assets by commodity, location, cost position, development stage, and financial metrics
- **Infrastructure Mapping**: View how assets relate to critical infrastructure including power plants, railways, and ports
- **Interactive Visualisation**: Explore assets through maps, charts, and customisable dashboards

### Use Cases
- Identify acquisition targets based on cost curve positioning
- Benchmark existing operations against global peers
- Screen greenfield and brownfield project opportunities
- Assess competitive landscape by region or commodity

### Commodities Supported
All 15 commodities: Copper, Aluminium, Gold, Nickel, Lead, Zinc, Lithium, Cobalt, Graphite, Rare Earths, Coal, Iron Ore, Steel, Bulk Steel Alloys, Noble Steel Alloys

---

## 2. Assets Valuations

### Purpose
Run post-tax financial metrics to understand the value of an asset. Use your own assumptions to run valuation models.

### Key Capabilities
- **Valuation Models**: Calculate NPV, IRR, payback period, and other key financial metrics
- **Custom Assumptions**: Input your own price forecasts, cost assumptions, discount rates, and tax parameters
- **Scenario Analysis**: Run multiple valuation scenarios to understand value drivers and sensitivities
- **Asset-Level Detail**: Model individual assets with detailed production profiles, cost structures, and reserve estimates
- **Post-Tax Analysis**: Comprehensive tax modelling reflecting jurisdictional variations

### Use Cases
- Evaluate potential acquisitions or divestments
- Assess project viability under different price and cost scenarios
- Model the impact of operational improvements on asset value
- Support investment committee decisions with robust financial analysis

### Commodities Supported
All 15 commodities with asset-level granularity

---

## 3. Markets Forecast

### Purpose
Comprehensive analysis of key drivers affecting the metals market. Access detailed long-term outlooks, pricing forecasts, and key insights across the metals and mining value chain.

### Key Capabilities
- **Long-Term Forecasts**: Supply, demand, and price forecasts extending to 2050
- **Market Balances**: Detailed supply-demand balances by commodity and region
- **Price Forecasting**: 40+ years of historical price data with forecasts for all covered commodities
- **Short-Term Outlook**: Monthly market outlook covering supply, demand, trade, and price forecasts for the next 2 years
- **Macroeconomic Integration**: Wood Mackenzie proprietary GDP, industrial production, inflation, and FX forecasts incorporated into commodity outlooks

### Use Cases
- Inform strategic planning with long-term market outlooks
- Support budget planning and price assumptions
- Evaluate market entry or exit timing
- Prepare investor presentations with authoritative market data

### Commodities Supported
All 15 commodities with comprehensive market coverage

---

## 4. Markets Scenarios

### Purpose
Compare key market drivers under different assumptions between Wood Mackenzie's base case and alternative energy transition scenarios.

### Key Capabilities
- **Base Case Scenario**: Wood Mackenzie's core outlook reflecting current policy and market trends
- **Energy Transition Scenarios**: Alternative pathways showing how accelerated decarbonisation affects commodity demand
- **Scenario Comparison Tools**: Side-by-side analysis of market drivers under different assumptions
- **Demand Impact Analysis**: Understand how EV adoption, renewable energy deployment, and policy changes affect metal demand
- **Supply Response Modelling**: Assess how supply may respond to different demand trajectories

### Use Cases
- Stress-test portfolios against different energy transition pathways
- Evaluate the impact of climate policies on commodity demand
- Identify commodities most exposed to energy transition risks and opportunities
- Support ESG and climate-related financial disclosures (TCFD)

### Focus Areas
Particularly valuable for battery metals (Lithium, Cobalt, Nickel, Graphite), Rare Earths, Copper, and Steel as these commodities are most exposed to energy transition dynamics.

---

## 5. Supply Chain

### Purpose
Assess material flows from mines to smelters to refineries, and understand the risk associated with the location of assets across the value chain.

### Key Capabilities
- **Material Flow Tracking**: Follow commodity flows from extraction through processing to end markets
- **Value Chain Mapping**: Visualise the entire supply chain from mine to market
- **Risk Assessment**: Evaluate geopolitical, operational, and logistical risks at each supply chain node
- **Infrastructure Analysis**: Map critical infrastructure dependencies (power, rail, ports)
- **Concentration Analysis**: Identify supply chain bottlenecks and single points of failure

### Use Cases
- Assess supply chain resilience and identify vulnerabilities
- Evaluate the impact of geopolitical events on material flows
- Plan procurement strategies with visibility into upstream supply chains
- Support due diligence on supply chain ESG risks
- Identify opportunities for vertical integration

### Commodities Supported
All 15 commodities with supply chain visualisation and risk assessment capabilities

## Solution Integration

These five solutions are designed to work together seamlessly:

```
Assets Discovery --> Assets Valuations --> Markets Forecast --> Markets Scenarios --> Supply Chain
     |                    |                      |                      |                |
     +--------------------+----------------------+----------------------+----------------+
                                    |
                          Integrated Decision Intelligence
```

Users can:
- Start with **Assets Discovery** to identify opportunities
- Run **Assets Valuations** to quantify potential returns
- Layer in **Markets Forecast** for market context
- Test against **Markets Scenarios** for risk assessment
- Validate **Supply Chain** integrity and resilience

This integrated approach ensures that decisions are informed by comprehensive, cross-functional analysis spanning the entire metals and mining value chain.

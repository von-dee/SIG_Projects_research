## 2. How does Lens Metals & Mining address changing demand and supply patterns in the industry?

Lens Metals & Mining enables users to derive insights from the supply-demand forecast when market developments or industry changes occur.

**Key capabilities include:**
- Assess supply and demand projections of total metals consumption
- Analyse direct-use scrap markets
- Review first-use and end-use demand by sector
- Compare Wood Mackenzie's authored outlooks to understand the market drivers behind the differences
- Access monthly short-term outlooks for rapid response to market changes
- Layer macroeconomic context (GDP, industrial production, inflation, FX) into commodity analysis

## 5. Why do I need macroeconomic forecasts for metals and mining analysis?

Commodity prices and demand don't exist in a vacuum. They're driven by:
- Economic growth
- Industrial activity
- Currency movements
- Inflationary pressures

Wood Mackenzie's proprietary macroeconomics data allows you to:
- Layer economic context into your analysis
- Identify risks earlier through leading economic indicators
- Make more informed strategic decisions based on economic cycles
- Forecast commodity demand based on GDP and industrial production trends
- Account for currency impacts on costs and revenues

## 6. Is Lens available via API?

Yes. **Lens Direct** is Wood Mackenzie's data delivery service that provides a feed connecting users to our extensive datasets using an API.

**Lens Direct capabilities:**
- Download Wood Mackenzie's data into your own proprietary systems
- Integrate with major BI tools including:
  - Microsoft Excel
  - Microsoft Power BI
  - TIBCO Spotfire
  - Tableau
- Automate data refreshes for always-current analysis
- Export time-series data, asset-level forecasts, cost curves, and financial metrics
- Seamlessly incorporate Lens data into existing workflows and models

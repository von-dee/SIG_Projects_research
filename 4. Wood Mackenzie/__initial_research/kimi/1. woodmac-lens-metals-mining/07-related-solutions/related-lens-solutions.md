# Related Lens Solutions

Wood Mackenzie Lens is an integrated platform of data analytics solutions spanning the entire energy and natural resources value chain. Lens Metals & Mining users can benefit from connecting insights across these complementary solutions.

---

## Lens Power & Renewables

**Purpose**: Tackle the energy transition head on and maximise investment opportunities in clean energy with an integrated view of global renewable and conventional power data and insights across projects, technologies and markets.

**Connection to Metals & Mining**: Electric vehicle and battery storage deployment drives demand for battery metals (lithium, cobalt, nickel, graphite). Renewable energy infrastructure requires copper, aluminium, and steel. Understanding power market developments is critical for forecasting metals demand.

---

## Lens Hydrogen

**Purpose**: Maximise opportunities across the hydrogen and ammonia value chain with Wood Mackenzie Lens Hydrogen. Identify, screen, and benchmark the hydrogen economy worldwide.

**Connection to Metals & Mining**: Hydrogen production (particularly green hydrogen) requires significant quantities of metals including platinum group metals for electrolysers, steel for infrastructure, and copper for electrical systems. Understanding hydrogen market development informs long-term metals demand forecasts.

---

## Lens Upstream

**Purpose**: Industry intelligence that enables you to minimise risk, identify upside, and optimise resources faster than anyone else.

**Connection to Metals & Mining**: Oil and gas extraction requires steel, nickel alloys, and other specialised metals. Understanding upstream activity levels provides demand signals for OCTG (oil country tubular goods) and other oilfield metals.

---

## Lens Subsurface

**Purpose**: Integrated commercial and technical upstream data for key portfolio insights.

**Connection to Metals & Mining**: Subsurface analysis techniques and data integration approaches can inform geological understanding for mineral exploration and resource estimation.

---

## Lens Lower 48

**Purpose**: Evaluate opportunity, streamline analysis, and maximise performance in the Lower 48.

**Connection to Metals & Mining**: US onshore oil and gas activity drives demand for steel pipe, proppants (which contain silica sand), and other mined commodities. Understanding Lower 48 activity levels informs regional metals demand.

---

## Wood Mackenzie Lens Carbon

**Purpose**: Source-to-sink evaluation of the carbon value chain.

**Connection to Metals & Mining**: Carbon pricing and emissions trading directly impact the cost structure of metals production, particularly for energy-intensive commodities like aluminium and steel. Understanding carbon market developments is essential for cost forecasting and scenario analysis.

---

## Lens Gas & LNG

**Purpose**: Gain a holistic view of the Gas and LNG industry with our comprehensive data solution.

**Connection to Metals & Mining**: LNG infrastructure requires significant quantities of nickel alloys, steel, and aluminium. Natural gas is also a key energy input and feedstock for metals production. Understanding gas market dynamics informs energy cost assumptions for smelters and refineries.

---

## Lens Direct

**Purpose**: The industry standard for robust data delivery that empowers your Lens access.

**Connection to Metals & Mining**: Lens Direct provides the API and data delivery infrastructure that enables seamless integration of Lens Metals & Mining data into your proprietary systems and BI tools (Excel, Power BI, Spotfire, Tableau).

---

## Lens Emissions

**Purpose**: Decision intelligence to simplify the complexity of emissions risk management. Available with Lens Upstream solutions.

**Connection to Metals & Mining**: Emissions tracking and carbon intensity analysis are increasingly important for metals and mining companies facing Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions reporting requirements. Understanding emissions profiles across the value chain supports ESG strategy and climate risk disclosure.

---

## Integration Benefits

By connecting Lens Metals & Mining with complementary Lens solutions, users gain:

1. **End-to-End Value Chain Visibility**: From resource extraction through processing to end-use applications
2. **Demand Signal Detection**: Early identification of demand drivers from energy transition developments
3. **Integrated Scenario Analysis**: Consistent assumptions across energy, chemicals, and metals in scenario modelling
4. **Risk Management**: Comprehensive view of risks spanning commodity, operational, and transition risks
5. **Competitive Advantage**: Cross-sector insights that siloed analysis cannot provide

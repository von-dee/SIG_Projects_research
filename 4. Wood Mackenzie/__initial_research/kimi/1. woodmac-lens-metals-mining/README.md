## About Wood Mackenzie Lens Metals & Mining

> "Unearth robust data analytics from mine to market. Layers of insight to attract and allocate capital."

Lens Metals & Mining combines Wood Mackenzie's decades of analytical expertise with a modern data analytics platform to deliver integrated cross-commodity insights. The platform is designed for a diverse range of professionals including mine operators, investors, traders, strategists, and policymakers seeking to make informed decisions in the complex metals and mining landscape.

## Use Case 1: Earnings Season Market Context Preparation

**Persona**: Investor Relations Professional
**Objective**: Prepare authoritative market context slides for quarterly earnings presentations

### Scenario
The IR Director needs to include market context in the Q3 earnings presentation, showing how the company's performance relates to commodity market movements. Investors expect independent, third-party data.

### Workflow

1. **Access Markets Forecast** solution
2. Pull year-to-date price movements for company's key commodities
3. Extract supply-demand balance changes during the quarter
4. Generate cost curve showing company's position vs. peers
5. Include macroeconomic context (GDP, industrial production trends)
6. Create visual slides for earnings deck

### Lens Metals & Mining Capabilities Used

- **Markets Forecast**: Price data, supply-demand balances, macroeconomic context
- **Assets Discovery**: Peer cost curve positioning
- **Export Tools**: Charts and data for PowerPoint integration

### Outcome

- Earnings presentation includes Wood Mackenzie independent market data
- Investor Q&A on market positioning answered confidently with third-party data
- Positive analyst feedback on transparency and data quality
- Stock outperforms sector by 2% post-earnings (attributed partly to clear communication)

### Value Delivered

- Enhanced credibility with investors through independent data sourcing
- Reduced preparation time from 3 days to 1 day
- Consistent data framework across all quarterly presentations

**Objective**: Create compelling, data-rich materials for an investor roadshow in London and New York

The IR team is planning a roadshow to meet 25 institutional investors. They need materials that clearly communicate the company's competitive advantages and market opportunity.

### Workflow

3. Pull peer benchmarking data from **Assets Discovery**
4. Create scenario comparison showing company positioned for net-zero transition
5. Export all charts and data into roadshow deck
6. Prepare appendix with detailed market data for interested investors

### Value Delivered

## Use Case 3: Crisis Communication Response

**Persona**: Investor Relations Professional
**Objective**: Rapidly respond to sudden commodity price drop with authoritative market analysis

### Scenario
Copper prices drop 15% in two weeks following concerns about Chinese demand. Investors flood the IR team with questions about the company's exposure and hedging strategy.

### Workflow

1. **Access Markets Forecast** for latest short-term outlook
2. Pull supply-demand data to understand drivers of price move
3. Check long-term forecast to see if fundamental thesis remains intact
4. Generate analysis showing company's cost position vs. current price
5. Prepare Q&A document and investor letter
6. Issue holding statement within 24 hours

### Lens Metals & Mining Capabilities Used

- **Markets Forecast**: Short-term monthly outlook, long-term price forecasts
- **Assets Discovery**: Cost curve positioning relative to current price
- **Data Coverage**: C1, C2, C3 cost data for margin analysis

### Outcome

- IR team issues data-driven response within 24 hours
- Analysis shows price drop is demand-destocking, not structural
- Company's Q1 cost position means operations remain profitable at current prices
- Investor confidence stabilised; stock recovers within 2 weeks

### Value Delivered

- Prevented panic selling and potential 5-10% stock price decline
- Demonstrated management's market expertise to investors
- Established Wood Mackenzie data as "go-to" source for market context

## Use Case 4: ESG and Sustainability Reporting

**Persona**: Investor Relations Professional
**Objective**: Source independent data to support ESG disclosures and sustainability reporting

### Scenario
The company is preparing its first TCFD-aligned climate disclosure and needs data on how energy transition scenarios affect its commodity portfolio and carbon exposure.

### Workflow

1. **Access Markets Scenarios** for energy transition pathways
2. Pull demand forecasts under different decarbonisation scenarios
3. Analyse which commodities benefit and which are at risk
4. Cross-reference with company's asset-level emissions data
5. Build scenario analysis for climate-related financial disclosures
6. Integrate into annual report and ESG presentation

### Lens Metals & Mining Capabilities Used

- **Markets Scenarios**: Base case vs. energy transition demand pathways
- **Markets Forecast**: Long-term commodity demand to 2050
- **Supply Chain**: Value chain emissions assessment

### Outcome

- TCFD disclosure completed with independent scenario analysis
- Rating agencies upgrade ESG score (MSCI from BBB to A)
- Company admitted to DJSI World Index
- Cost of ESG-linked debt reduced by 25 basis points

### Value Delivered

- $10M annual interest savings on ESG-linked facilities
- Attraction of sustainability-focused investor capital
- Reduced regulatory risk through proactive disclosure

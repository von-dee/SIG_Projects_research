## Use Case 1: Mining Equity Valuation Model

**Persona**: Investment Analyst
**Objective**: Build a comprehensive valuation model for a copper mining company using independent commodity forecasts

### Scenario
A sell-side analyst is initiating coverage on a mid-tier copper producer and needs to build a DCF model with defensible commodity price assumptions.

### Workflow

1. **Access Markets Forecast** for copper long-term price forecast
2. Extract Wood Mackenzie's copper supply-demand balance to 2050
3. **Access Assets Valuations** to input asset-level production and cost data
4. Build DCF model with independent price deck
5. Run sensitivity analysis on copper price and costs
6. Publish initiation report with price target

### Lens Metals & Mining Capabilities Used

- **Markets Forecast**: Copper price forecast to 2050, supply-demand data
- **Assets Valuations**: Asset-level production profiles, cost structures
- **Data Coverage**: C1, C2, C3 costs, reserve data

### Outcome

- Initiation report features independent Wood Mackenzie price deck
- Price target based on $3.80/lb long-term copper (vs. spot $4.20/lb)
- Stock rated "Buy" with 25% upside to target
- Report cited by 12 other analysts; company added to several fund portfolios

### Value Delivered

- Differentiated research with independent commodity views
- Client funds generate 20% return over 12 months
- Analyst ranking improves (Institutional Investor #3 to #1 in sector)

## Use Case 2: Commodity Top-Down Sector Allocation

**Persona**: Investment Analyst
**Objective**: Recommend sector weightings across mining commodities based on supply-demand fundamentals

### Scenario
A market strategist at a fund needs to recommend commodity allocation across copper, gold, lithium, and iron ore for the next 12 months.

### Workflow

1. **Access Markets Forecast** for all four commodities
2. Pull supply-demand balances and price forecasts
3. Check inventories and market balances
4. Review project pipeline for supply additions
5. Analyse macroeconomic drivers (China stimulus, Fed policy, USD)
6. Rank commodities by fundamental attractiveness

### Lens Metals & Mining Capabilities Used

- **Markets Forecast**: Supply-demand balances, price forecasts, trade flows
- **Data Coverage**: Project pipeline data, cost curve positioning
- **Markets Scenarios**: Macro scenario analysis

### Outcome

Recommendation:
- **Overweight Copper**: Supply deficits expected; low inventory
- **Neutral Gold**: Rate uncertainty supports prices but limited upside
- **Overweight Lithium**: Structural deficit despite recent price correction
- **Underweight Iron Ore**: Chinese property slowdown; new supply coming

Fund rotates $200M according to recommendations

### Value Delivered

- Fund outperforms benchmark by 8% over 12 months
- Overweight copper and lithium positions generate 35% and 50% returns respectively
- Underweight iron ore avoids 15% decline

## Use Case 3: M&A Situation Analysis

**Persona**: Investment Analyst
**Objective**: Analyse a proposed mining merger and assess the strategic rationale and valuation

### Scenario
Two gold miners announce a merger of equals. The analyst needs to assess whether the deal creates value and what the combined entity's cost curve position looks like.

### Workflow

1. **Access Assets Discovery** for both companies' assets
2. Generate combined cost curve showing merged entity vs. peers
3. Pull AISC data for all assets
4. **Access Assets Valuations** to model synergies and combined NPV
5. Compare to other gold sector M&A transactions
6. Publish merger analysis with recommendation

### Lens Metals & Mining Capabilities Used

- **Assets Discovery**: Peer benchmarking, cost curve generation
- **Assets Valuations**: NPV modelling with synergy assumptions
- **Data Coverage**: AISC curves, reserve data, production profiles

### Outcome

Analysis shows:
- Combined entity moves from 3rd to 1st quartile on AISC curve
- $150M annual synergies achievable
- Deal is 15% accretive to NAV
- Analyst recommends shareholders vote FOR the merger

### Value Delivered

- Clients vote appropriately, realising merger premium
- Synergies exceed analyst estimates by 20% in first year
- Combined stock outperforms gold index by 12%

## Use Case 4: Battery Metals Thematic Research

**Persona**: Investment Analyst
**Objective**: Produce a thematic research piece on the battery metals investment opportunity

### Scenario
A research director requests a comprehensive report on the lithium, cobalt, nickel, and graphite investment landscape for EV batteries.

### Workflow

1. **Access Markets Forecast** for all four battery metals
2. Pull demand forecasts to 2050 with EV adoption scenarios
3. Review project pipeline for each commodity
4. **Access Markets Scenarios** for different EV penetration rates
5. Generate supply-demand balances under each scenario
6. Identify supply gaps and investment opportunities

### Lens Metals & Mining Capabilities Used

- **Markets Forecast**: Long-term demand forecasts, project pipeline data
- **Markets Scenarios**: EV penetration scenario analysis
- **Assets Discovery**: Project screening and cost analysis

### Outcome

- 40-page thematic report published
- Identifies 5 "buy" rated stocks positioned for battery metals demand
- Report generates 3,000 downloads in first month
- Hosts webinar with 500+ attendees

### Value Delivered

- Top-rated stock (lithium producer) returns 80% over 12 months
- Research team wins "Thematic Research Award" at industry conference
- $50M in new AUM attributed to battery metals theme

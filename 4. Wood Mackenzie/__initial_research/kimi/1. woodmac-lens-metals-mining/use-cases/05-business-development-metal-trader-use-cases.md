## Use Case 1: Long-Term offtake Contract Negotiation

**Persona**: Business Development & Metal Trader
**Objective**: Negotiate a 5-year copper concentrate offtake agreement with favourable pricing terms

### Scenario
A commodity trader is negotiating a 5-year offtake contract for 100,000 tonnes per annum of copper concentrate from a new mine in Peru. Need to determine fair pricing and volume flexibilities.

### Workflow

1. **Access Markets Forecast** for copper long-term price outlook
2. Analyse concentrate treatment and refining charge (TC/RC) trends
3. Review project's cost position on global cost curve
4. Assess smelter capacity availability in target markets
5. Model contract economics under different price scenarios
6. Negotiate with data-backed position

### Lens Metals & Mining Capabilities Used

- **Markets Forecast**: Long-term copper price forecasts, TC/RC trends
- **Assets Discovery**: Mine cost position and project details
- **Supply Chain**: Smelter capacity and logistics analysis

### Outcome

- Negotiated pricing formula with floor and ceiling protections
- Secured volume flexibility (±15%) based on market conditions
- Contract valued at $2.5B over 5 years
- Margin locked in at $200M expected profit

### Value Delivered

- Data-backed negotiation prevented overpayment of $50M
- Secured long-term supply relationship
- Volume flexibility protects against demand fluctuations

## Use Case 2: Regional Arbitrage Opportunity Identification

**Persona**: Metal Trader
**Objective**: Identify and execute a profitable arbitrage trade between copper markets

### Scenario
A trader notices a widening price differential between copper cathode in Shanghai and London Metal Exchange (LME) warehouses. Need to assess whether the spread is sufficient to cover logistics and financing costs.

### Workflow

1. **Access Markets Forecast** for regional price differentials
2. **Access Supply Chain** for trade flow and logistics cost data
3. Check freight rates (Shanghai to Rotterdam)
4. Calculate financing and storage costs
5. Assess import/export duty implications
6. Execute trade if spread exceeds cost threshold

### Lens Metals & Mining Capabilities Used

- **Markets Forecast**: Regional price data and trade flow analysis
- **Supply Chain**: Logistics routes, freight cost benchmarks
- **Data Coverage**: Trade flow and inventory data

### Outcome

- Spread analysis shows $280/t gross differential
- Logistics and financing costs total $180/t
- Net profit: $100/t on 5,000 tonnes = $500,000
- Trade executed; profit realised in 6 weeks

### Value Delivered

- $500,000 profit on single trade
- Identified recurring arbitrage opportunity generating $2M annually
- Enhanced trading desk's regional expertise

## Use Case 3: New Market Entry Feasibility

**Persona**: Business Development Manager
**Objective**: Assess the feasibility of entering the European battery metals trading market

### Scenario
A trading house is considering expanding into European battery metals (lithium, cobalt, nickel) to serve the growing EV manufacturing sector. Need to assess market size, competition, and logistics requirements.

### Workflow

1. **Access Markets Forecast** for European battery metals demand to 2035
2. Analyse EV production plans for European OEMs
3. **Access Supply Chain** to map supply routes and logistics infrastructure
4. Identify key consumers and competitors
5. Assess regulatory requirements (EU Critical Raw Materials Act)
6. Build business case with market sizing

### Lens Metals & Mining Capabilities Used

- **Markets Forecast**: Battery metals demand forecasts by region
- **Supply Chain**: European logistics and port infrastructure data
- **Markets Scenarios**: EV adoption scenario analysis

### Outcome

Business case shows:
- European lithium demand to grow 8x by 2035
- Cobalt demand to triple
- Nickel demand for batteries to increase 5x
- Market opportunity valued at $500M annual revenue potential

### Value Delivered

- Board approves European battery metals trading launch
- $50M investment in logistics and inventory
- Captures 10% market share within 3 years
- Positions company for long-term growth in strategic market

## Use Case 4: Inventory Optimisation and Working Capital Management

**Persona**: Metal Trader
**Objective**: Optimise zinc inventory levels to balance supply security with working capital efficiency

### Scenario
A trader holds 20,000 tonnes of zinc inventory across multiple warehouses. Working capital constraints require reducing inventory while maintaining supply commitments to customers.

### Workflow

1. **Access Markets Forecast** for zinc short-term supply-demand outlook
2. Check inventory levels globally and in target regions
3. Analyse demand patterns for key customers
4. Review logistics lead times from alternative sources
5. Model inventory scenarios (15,000t, 10,000t, 5,000t)
6. Optimise inventory position

### Lens Metals & Mining Capabilities Used

- **Markets Forecast**: Zinc supply-demand balance, inventory data
- **Supply Chain**: Logistics lead times and routing options
- **Data Coverage**: Warehouse inventory and availability

### Outcome

- Market analysis shows comfortable supply outlook
- Inventory reduced from 20,000t to 12,000t
- Working capital released: $25M (at $3,000/t zinc price)
- Supply commitments maintained with 2-week safety stock
- Freed capital deployed into higher-margin trading opportunities

### Value Delivered

- $25M working capital released
- $1.5M annual carrying cost savings
- Higher return on capital employed (ROCE improves 2 percentage points)

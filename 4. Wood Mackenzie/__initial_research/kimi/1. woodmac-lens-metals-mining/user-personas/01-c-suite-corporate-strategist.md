# User Persona: C-Suite & Corporate Strategist

## Persona Overview

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Role** | C-Suite Executive / Corporate Strategy Director |
| **Industry** | Mining / Metals / Diversified Resources |
| **Experience Level** | 15+ years |
| **Reports To** | Board of Directors / CEO |
| **Direct Reports** | Strategy team, Business Development, M&A |

## Demographics

- **Age Range**: 45-60
- **Education**: MBA, Engineering, or Economics background
- **Location**: Corporate headquarters (major mining hubs: Melbourne, London, Toronto, Johannesburg, São Paulo)
- **Work Style**: High-level strategic focus, board presentations, capital allocation decisions

## Goals & Objectives

1. **Portfolio Optimisation**: Ensure the company's asset portfolio is positioned for long-term value creation
2. **Capital Allocation**: Deploy capital to the highest-return opportunities across commodities and geographies
3. **Competitive Positioning**: Maintain or improve competitive position against global peers
4. **Risk Management**: Identify and mitigate strategic risks including energy transition, geopolitical, and market risks
5. **Stakeholder Value**: Deliver sustainable returns to shareholders while managing ESG expectations
6. **Growth Strategy**: Identify and evaluate organic and inorganic growth opportunities

## Pain Points

1. **Information Overload**: Difficult to synthesise data from multiple sources into actionable strategic insights
2. **Long-Term Uncertainty**: Energy transition creates unprecedented uncertainty in demand forecasts
3. **Cross-Commodity Complexity**: Hard to compare opportunities across different commodities with different market dynamics
4. **Scenario Planning**: Limited ability to model multiple future scenarios systematically
5. **Peer Benchmarking**: Lack of standardised data for meaningful peer comparisons
6. **Board Communication**: Need to distil complex analysis into clear, compelling board presentations

## Technology Profile

- Uses dashboards and executive summaries rather than raw data
- Relies on strategy teams and analysts for detailed analysis
- Prefers visual, interactive tools for board presentations
- Needs mobile-accessible insights for travel-heavy schedule
- Integrates data into PowerPoint and board packs

## Key Questions This Persona Needs Answered

1. Where does our portfolio sit on global cost curves?
2. Which commodities offer the best risk-adjusted returns over the next decade?
3. How will energy transition scenarios affect our asset valuations?
4. Which peers are we outperforming or underperforming?
5. Where should we allocate our next dollar of capital?
6. What M&A targets would strengthen our competitive position?

## Preferred Solutions

- **Assets Discovery**: Portfolio benchmarking and competitive positioning
- **Assets Valuations**: Strategic asset valuation and scenario testing
- **Markets Scenarios**: Energy transition scenario analysis for long-term planning
- **Markets Forecast**: High-level market outlook for commodity allocation decisions

## Quote

> "I need to see the big picture across all our commodities and geographies, with confidence that the data supports our strategic decisions. The board expects me to have answers about where the industry is heading."

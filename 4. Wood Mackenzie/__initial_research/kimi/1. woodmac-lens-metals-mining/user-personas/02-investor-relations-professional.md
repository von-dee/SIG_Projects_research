# User Persona: Investor Relations Professional

## Persona Overview

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Role** | Investor Relations Manager / Director |
| **Industry** | Mining / Metals / Listed Resources Companies |
| **Experience Level** | 8-15 years |
| **Reports To** | CFO / Head of Corporate Affairs |
| **Direct Reports** | IR Analysts, Communications team |

## Demographics

- **Age Range**: 32-50
- **Education**: Finance, Communications, or Business degree; CFA or MBA common
- **Location**: Corporate headquarters or financial centres (London, New York, Toronto, Sydney)
- **Work Style**: Investor-facing, presentation-heavy, earnings cycle driven

## Goals & Objectives

1. **Shareholder Communication**: Clearly articulate company strategy and performance to investors
2. **Market Positioning**: Ensure the investment community understands the company's value proposition
3. **Feedback Loop**: Gather investor sentiment and concerns for management
4. **Crisis Communication**: Respond quickly to market events or operational issues
5. **ESG Storytelling**: Communicate sustainability and ESG initiatives effectively
6. **Valuation Support**: Provide data that supports fair market valuation of the company

## Pain Points

1. **Data Reconciliation**: Difficult to ensure consistency between internal forecasts and external market data
2. **Competitor Benchmarking**: Hard to access standardised peer data for comparison
3. **Scenario Explanation**: Complex to explain how different market scenarios affect the company
4. **Rapid Response**: Need quick access to authoritative data for ad-hoc investor queries
5. **Credibility**: Need third-party, independent data sources to support company narratives
6. **ESG Data**: Growing demand for ESG and decarbonisation data that's hard to compile

## Technology Profile

- Heavy user of PowerPoint and Excel for presentations
- Needs data visualisation tools for investor materials
- Uses CRM systems to track investor interactions
- Requires access to real-time market data during roadshows
- Consumes research reports and market analysis regularly

## Key Questions This Persona Needs Answered

1. How does our cost position compare to our peers?
2. What are the current supply-demand dynamics in our key commodities?
3. How do energy transition scenarios affect our commodity exposure?
4. What are analyst consensus forecasts versus Wood Mackenzie's independent view?
5. How can we demonstrate portfolio resilience to investors?
6. What market data supports our strategic narrative?

## Preferred Solutions

- **Markets Forecast**: Authoritative market outlooks to support investor communications
- **Markets Scenarios**: Scenario analysis to demonstrate portfolio resilience
- **Assets Discovery**: Peer benchmarking data for competitive positioning

## Quote

> "I need credible, independent market data that I can share with investors with confidence. When an investor asks about the copper market outlook, I need the answer at my fingertips."

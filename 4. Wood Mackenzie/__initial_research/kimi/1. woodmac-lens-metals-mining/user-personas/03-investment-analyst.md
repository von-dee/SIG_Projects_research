# User Persona: Investment Analyst / Market Analyst

## Persona Overview

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Role** | Mining Equity Analyst / Commodity Market Analyst |
| **Industry** | Investment Banking / Asset Management / Sell-Side Research |
| **Experience Level** | 5-12 years |
| **Reports To** | Head of Research / Portfolio Manager |
| **Direct Reports** | Junior analysts (in some cases) |

## Demographics

- **Age Range**: 28-45
- **Education**: Finance, Economics, Engineering, or Geology; CFA common
- **Location**: Financial centres (London, New York, Toronto, Sydney, Hong Kong, Singapore)
- **Work Style**: Deep analytical work, financial modelling, report writing, client interaction

## Goals & Objectives

1. **Generate Investment Ideas**: Identify mispriced mining equities and commodities
2. **Accurate Forecasting**: Develop reliable commodity price and company earnings forecasts
3. **Valuation Models**: Build robust financial models for mining company coverage
4. **Sector Coverage**: Maintain comprehensive knowledge across multiple commodities
5. **Client Service**: Provide timely, actionable insights to portfolio managers and clients
6. **Alpha Generation**: Find information advantages that lead to better investment returns

## Pain Points

1. **Data Fragmentation**: Must gather data from multiple disparate sources (company reports, trade associations, government statistics)
2. **Model Maintenance**: Keeping complex financial models updated with latest data is time-consuming
3. **Assumption Validation**: Difficult to verify the reasonableness of company guidance and market assumptions
4. **Cross-Commodity Analysis**: Hard to compare investment opportunities across different commodities
5. **Scenario Modelling**: Limited tools for systematic scenario analysis
6. **Time Pressure**: Need to respond quickly to market events and company announcements

## Technology Profile

- Expert Excel user with complex financial models
- Uses Bloomberg, Refinitiv, or FactSet for market data
- Consumes research reports and industry publications extensively
- Builds custom models in Excel or specialised software
- Needs API access for automated data feeds into models

## Key Questions This Persona Needs Answered

1. What is the fair value of this mining company under different price scenarios?
2. How does this company's cost position compare to global peers?
3. What are the supply-demand fundamentals driving commodity prices?
4. How will energy transition affect long-term demand for this commodity?
5. Which projects in the pipeline could significantly change supply dynamics?
6. What are the key sensitivities in my valuation model?

## Preferred Solutions

- **Assets Valuations**: Run independent valuations with custom assumptions
- **Markets Forecast**: Supply-demand balances and price forecasts for model inputs
- **Markets Scenarios**: Scenario analysis for stress-testing investment theses
- **Assets Discovery**: Asset screening and peer comparison
- **Supply Chain**: Supply chain risk assessment for covered companies

## Quote

> "I need accurate, granular data that I can plug directly into my models. The ability to run my own scenarios with independent supply-demand forecasts gives me a real edge in generating investment ideas."

# User Persona: Fund Manager

## Persona Overview

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Role** | Portfolio Manager / Fund Manager (Mining & Resources Focus) |
| **Industry** | Asset Management / Hedge Funds / Pension Funds |
| **Experience Level** | 10-20 years |
| **Reports To** | CIO / Head of Equities or Commodities |
| **Direct Reports** | Analysts, Traders |

## Demographics

- **Age Range**: 35-55
- **Education**: MBA, CFA, or Economics; often with technical background
- **Location**: Major financial centres (London, New York, Boston, Sydney, Hong Kong)
- **Work Style**: Decision-focused, risk-aware, performance-driven

## Goals & Objectives

1. **Generate Returns**: Outperform benchmarks through superior stock selection and timing
2. **Risk Management**: Manage portfolio risk across commodities, geographies, and currencies
3. **Asset Allocation**: Determine optimal weighting across mining sub-sectors
4. **Idea Generation**: Source differentiated investment ideas from comprehensive data
5. **Due Diligence**: Conduct thorough analysis before making investment decisions
6. **Portfolio Construction**: Build diversified portfolios with appropriate risk-return profiles

## Pain Points

1. **Conviction Building**: Hard to develop high-conviction views with confidence
2. **Data Quality**: Concerned about the quality and independence of data sources
3. **Time Scarcity**: Too much information, not enough time to process it
4. **Model Risk**: Uncertainty about the assumptions underlying company and market forecasts
5. **Crowded Trades**: Difficulty finding differentiated insights in well-covered sectors
6. **ESG Integration**: Need to integrate ESG factors into investment process systematically

## Technology Profile

- Uses portfolio management and risk systems (e.g., Bloomberg AIM, Aladdin)
- Reviews analyst models and research reports
- Prefers executive summaries with ability to drill down
- Needs real-time alerts for portfolio-relevant events
- Uses data visualisation for portfolio-level analysis

## Key Questions This Persona Needs Answered

1. Which commodities are structurally attractive over my investment horizon?
2. Where are the best risk-adjusted opportunities in the mining sector?
3. How does energy transition create winners and losers in my portfolio?
4. What are the key downside risks to my mining positions?
5. Are current valuations pricing in realistic commodity price assumptions?
6. How can I diversify my commodity exposure effectively?

## Preferred Solutions

- **Markets Forecast**: Long-term market outlooks for strategic allocation
- **Markets Scenarios**: Scenario analysis for risk assessment and opportunity identification
- **Assets Discovery**: Screen for investment opportunities across the global mining universe
- **Assets Valuations**: Independent valuation checks on portfolio holdings

## Quote

> "I manage a concentrated portfolio of mining stocks. I need independent, high-conviction views on where commodity markets are heading. The ability to stress-test my positions against different scenarios is invaluable."

# User Persona: Business Development & Metal Trader

## Persona Overview

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Role** | Business Development Manager / Metal Trader / Commercial Manager |
| **Industry** | Mining / Metals Trading / Commodity Merchants |
| **Experience Level** | 7-15 years |
| **Reports To** | Head of Trading / Commercial Director / VP Business Development |
| **Direct Reports** | Trading assistants, analysts |

## Demographics

- **Age Range**: 30-50
- **Education**: Business, Finance, Engineering, or Economics; MBA common
- **Location**: Trading hubs (London, Geneva, Singapore, Shanghai, New York)
- **Work Style**: Fast-paced, deal-driven, market-focused, relationship-oriented

## Goals & Objectives

1. **Deal Execution**: Identify and execute profitable trading and business development opportunities
2. **Market Timing**: Enter and exit positions at optimal price points
3. **Relationship Building**: Maintain strong relationships with miners, consumers, and intermediaries
4. **Price Forecasting**: Access reliable price forecasts for contract negotiations
5. **Arbitrage Identification**: Find price differentials between markets, grades, and time periods
6. **Supply Security**: Ensure reliable supply sources for offtake agreements

## Pain Points

1. **Market Volatility**: Rapid price movements create risk and opportunity simultaneously
2. **Information Asymmetry**: Difficult to get timely, accurate market intelligence
3. **Contract Risk**: Long-term contracts expose traders to price and volume risks
4. **Counterparty Risk**: Need to assess the reliability of suppliers and customers
5. **Regulatory Complexity**: Trade restrictions, tariffs, and sanctions create compliance challenges
6. **Geopolitical Risk**: Political instability in producing regions disrupts supply chains

## Technology Profile

- Uses trading platforms and execution systems
- Needs real-time and near-real-time market data
- Builds pricing models in Excel
- Communicates frequently via messaging and voice
- Uses mobile devices for market monitoring

## Key Questions This Persona Needs Answered

1. What are the supply-demand trends for my key commodities?
2. Where are the regional arbitrage opportunities?
3. What are the price forecasts for contract negotiations?
4. Which new projects are coming online that could affect supply?
5. How are trade flows shifting between regions?
6. What is the cost floor for key producers (support levels)?

## Preferred Solutions

- **Markets Forecast**: Supply-demand trends and price forecasts for deal timing
- **Supply Chain**: Trade flow analysis and logistics intelligence
- **Assets Discovery**: Monitor project pipeline and production changes
- **Markets Scenarios**: Assess how different scenarios affect contract values

## Quote

> "I live and breathe market dynamics. I need to know where supply is coming from, where demand is heading, and where the price dislocations are. Every trading decision is only as good as the data behind it."

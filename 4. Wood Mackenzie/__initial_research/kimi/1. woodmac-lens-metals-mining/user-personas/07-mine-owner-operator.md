# User Persona: Mine Owner & Operator

## Persona Overview

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Role** | Mine Manager / Operations Director / Technical Services Manager |
| **Industry** | Mining Operations / Mine Technical Services |
| **Experience Level** | 12-25 years |
| **Reports To** | VP Operations / COO / Mine General Manager |
| **Direct Reports** | Engineering, Geology, Metallurgy, Maintenance teams |

## Demographics

- **Age Range**: 35-55
- **Education**: Mining Engineering, Geology, Metallurgy, or related technical degree
- **Location**: Mine sites and regional offices (often remote locations)
- **Work Style**: Operations-focused, safety-conscious, cost-driven, continuous improvement

## Goals & Objectives

1. **Cost Optimisation**: Reduce unit operating costs to improve margin and cost curve position
2. **Production Efficiency**: Maximise throughput while maintaining quality specifications
3. **Resource Optimisation**: Efficient extraction of reserves with minimal waste
4. **Safety Performance**: Maintain exemplary safety records and compliance
5. **Sustainability**: Minimise environmental impact and meet ESG commitments
6. **Reserve Replacement**: Support exploration and resource definition efforts

## Pain Points

1. **Cost Pressure**: Continuous pressure to reduce costs as margins fluctuate
2. **Benchmarking**: Difficult to access meaningful peer benchmarks for performance comparison
3. **Market Visibility**: Limited visibility into market dynamics that affect revenue
4. **Resource Uncertainty**: Geological uncertainty complicates planning and forecasting
5. **Skills Shortage**: Difficulty attracting and retaining technical talent
6. **Regulatory Burden**: Increasing compliance requirements across safety, environment, and reporting

## Technology Profile

- Uses mine planning software (e.g., Deswik, Vulcan, MineSight)
- Familiar with ERP and maintenance management systems
- Prefers practical, actionable data over theoretical analysis
- Uses dashboards for KPI monitoring
- Needs offline-capable tools for remote site access

## Key Questions This Persona Needs Answered

1. How does our cost position compare to similar operations globally?
2. What operational benchmarks should we target for improvement?
3. How are commodity prices trending and what does this mean for our margins?
4. Which cost drivers are most significant for our operation?
5. What is the incentive price required to justify expansion or new projects?
6. How are our peers performing on key operational metrics?

## Preferred Solutions

- **Assets Discovery**: Peer benchmarking and operational comparison
- **Markets Forecast**: Price outlook for budgeting and planning
- **Assets Valuations**: Assess the value of operational improvements
- **Data Coverage**: Detailed cost and operational benchmarks

## Quote

> "I run a mine, not a spreadsheet. I need practical data that helps me understand how we're performing against our peers and what the market outlook means for our operation. Show me where we can improve and what the prize is."

Based on my analysis of the Wood Mackenzie (woodmac.com) website, here is a breakdown of the software UI menus and page flows:

---

## Global Navigation (Header)

The primary navigation follows a **flat hierarchy** with these top-level items:

| Menu Item | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Products** | Data analytics platforms and solutions |
| **Industries** | Sector-specific research coverage |
| **Insights** | Reports, analysis, and thought leadership |
| **About** | Company information, locations, leadership |
| **Contact** | Expert contact forms and support |

---

## Primary Page Flows

### 1. Products → Lens Platform Ecosystem

The **Lens** platform is Wood Mackenzie's core data analytics product suite. The navigation flows as:

```
Products
├── Lens Upstream
│   ├── Asset Discovery & Screening
│   ├── Valuation & Modelling
│   ├── Portfolio Optimisation
│   └── Emissions & Decarbonisation
├── Lens Hydrogen
│   ├── Hydrogen Value Chain
│   ├── Ammonia & Methanol
│   ├── Carbon Capture Integration
│   └── Asset Valuations
├── Lens Carbon
│   ├── Carbon Markets
│   ├── Decarbonisation Pathways
│   └── Net-Zero Strategy
├── Lens Power & Renewables
├── Lens Gas & LNG
├── Lens Metals & Mining
│   ├── Copper, Aluminium, Gold, Nickel
```

---

## Key Page Flow Patterns

### Product Discovery Flow
1. **Homepage** → Value proposition ("Intelligence Connected")
2. **Products Overview** → Modular solution explorer
3. **Individual Lens Product** → Feature breakdown + Workflows + Demo/Contact CTA
4. **Contact Form** → Expert consultation request

### Research Access Flow
1. **Insights** → Filter by commodity/topic
2. **Article/Report Page** → Summary + Form-gated full content
3. **Download/Access** → Subscription or trial signup

### Data Integration Flow
1. **Lens Direct** → API documentation
2. **Excel Plug-in / BI Tools** → Power BI, Tableau, Spotfire integration
3. **Data Delivery** → FTP, S3, API endpoints

---

## Mobile Navigation Pattern

- **Hamburger menu** for full navigation on mobile
- **Sticky header** persists during scroll
- **Accordion-style** submenus for Lens product categories
- Touch-optimized CTAs for "Contact an expert"

---

## Notable UX Characteristics

- **Mega-menu structure** for Products dropdown (visual grouping of Lens solutions)
- **Consistent CTAs**: "Contact an expert" and "Explore" appear across product pages
- **Cross-linking**: Related Lens solutions promoted on individual product pages (e.g., Lens Hydrogen links to Lens Carbon and Lens Power & Renewables) 
- **Form-gated content**: Premium insights require contact information
- **Global support contacts** accessible via footer (EMEA, Americas, Asia-Pacific)

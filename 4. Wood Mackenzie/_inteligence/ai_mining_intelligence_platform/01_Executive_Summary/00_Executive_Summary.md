# Executive Summary

## Project: Minerva — AI-Powered Mining & Commodity Intelligence Platform

### Vision Statement
Build the world's first fully agentic, real-time mining and commodity intelligence platform that replaces the static, expensive, analyst-driven model of incumbents like Wood Mackenzie, S&P Global Commodity Insights, and CRU Group with an AI-native, conversational, continuously updating system at a fraction of the cost.

### The Problem
The global mining intelligence market is dominated by legacy research firms that operate on a 20th-century model:
- **Expensive**: Annual subscriptions cost $50K–$500K+ per seat
- **Static**: Reports published on quarterly cycles, not on demand
- **Siloed**: No integration with client internal data or workflows
- **Non-interactive**: Users read PDFs and navigate rigid dashboards
- **Slow**: Market events are analyzed days or weeks after they occur

The global data analytics market is projected to grow from $83.79 billion in 2026 to $785.62 billion by 2035 (CAGR 28.35%), with augmented analytics growing at the highest rate. The smart mining market alone is valued at $20.5 billion in 2026. Yet mining-specific intelligence remains stuck in the past.

### The Solution: Minerva
An AI-native platform that combines:
1. **Real-time data aggregation** from 50+ public and proprietary sources
2. **Conversational intelligence** — ask natural language questions, get analyst-grade answers
3. **Agentic alerting** — AI agents monitor conditions and proactively surface insights
4. **Asset-level cost modeling** — dynamic, stress-testable cost curves
5. **On-demand report generation** — AI-generated research grounded in real data
6. **Internal data integration** — blend public intelligence with private client data

### Key Differentiators
| Dimension | Wood Mackenzie / S&P Global | Minerva |
|---|---|---|
| Update Frequency | Quarterly reports | Real-time continuous |
| Interface | Static PDFs + dashboards | Conversational AI |
| Cost per Seat | $50K–$500K/year | $5K–$50K/year |
| Customization | Limited | Full — user-defined models |
| Internal Data | None | Native integration |
| Alerting | Manual monitoring | Agentic, proactive |
| Report Generation | Analyst schedule | On-demand, instant |

### Target Market
- **Primary**: Mining companies (Tier 1–3), commodity traders, investment funds
- **Secondary**: ESG consultancies, government mineral departments, equipment OEMs
- **Tertiary**: Academic institutions, NGOs, media

### Initial Vertical
**Lithium & Battery Metals** — fastest-growing commodity segment with the highest information asymmetry and the most price volatility. Serves as the beachhead before expanding to copper, nickel, cobalt, rare earths, and gold.

### Business Model
SaaS subscription with tiered pricing + enterprise consulting + API access + data licensing.

### Funding Ask
Seed round: $3–5M to build MVP, validate with 5–10 design partners, and achieve initial revenue.

### Team Requirements
- 2x Founding Engineers (AI/Backend, Frontend/UX)
- 1x Domain Expert (former mining analyst/trader)
- 1x Data Engineer
- 1x Product Manager
- 1x Sales/BD Lead

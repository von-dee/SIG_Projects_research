# Business Model

## Revenue Streams

### 1. SaaS Subscriptions (Primary — 70% of Revenue)

#### Tier Structure

| Tier | Name | Price/Month | Target | Features |
|------|------|-------------|--------|----------|
| **Free** | Explorer | $0 | Students, journalists, small investors | Limited queries, 3-day delayed data, basic chat |
| **Pro** | Analyst | $499/mo | Independent analysts, small funds | Real-time data, 500 queries/mo, 3 commodities, basic alerts |
| **Team** | Strategist | $2,499/mo | Mid-size funds, trading desks | Unlimited queries, 10 commodities, advanced alerts, API access (10K calls/mo), team collaboration |
| **Enterprise** | Director | Custom ($15K–$50K/mo) | Major miners, Tier-1 funds, trading houses | Full coverage, unlimited API, internal data integration, custom agents, dedicated support, white-label options |

#### Pricing Philosophy
- **10x cheaper** than Wood Mackenzie equivalents at comparable depth
- **Value-based pricing**: Enterprise clients pay based on portfolio AUM or production volume
- **Annual contracts** with 15–20% discount to improve retention

### 2. API & Data Licensing (15% of Revenue)
- **REST API** for real-time price feeds, cost curves, supply/demand data
- **Webhook subscriptions** for event-driven data pushes
- **Bulk data licensing** to fintech platforms, Bloomberg Terminal integrations, trading systems
- Pricing: $0.01–$0.50 per API call depending on data granularity

### 3. Consulting & Advisory (10% of Revenue)
- **Custom research reports** ($5K–$50K per report)
- **Due diligence support** for M&A transactions
- **Workshops & training** on commodity market intelligence
- **ESG benchmarking** and compliance reporting

### 4. Partnership Revenue (5% of Revenue)
- Revenue share with data providers, satellite imagery partners
- White-label platform licensing to regional commodity exchanges
- Co-branded research with industry associations

## Go-to-Market Economics

### Customer Acquisition Strategy
1. **Design Partner Program** (Months 0–12): 5–10 free seats in exchange for feedback and case studies
2. **Content Marketing**: Weekly AI-generated market briefs, blog posts, Twitter threads
3. **Conference Presence**: PDAC, LME Week, CRU World Copper, Benchmark Mineral Intelligence events
4. **Referral Program**: 20% discount for referring new enterprise clients
5. **Freemium Conversion**: Free tier → Pro via feature gating and data freshness

### Sales Motion
- **Self-serve** for Pro tier (credit card, instant activation)
- **Inside sales** for Team tier (demo, 2-week trial, annual contract)
- **Field sales** for Enterprise (C-suite engagement, POC, 6-month pilot)

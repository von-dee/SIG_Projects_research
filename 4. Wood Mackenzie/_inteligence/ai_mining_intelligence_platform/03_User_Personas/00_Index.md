# User Personas Index

## Overview
Minerva serves eight distinct user personas across the mining and commodity value chain. Each persona has unique workflows, pain points, and success metrics. The platform is designed to deliver value to all personas while prioritizing the highest-revenue segments.

## Persona Matrix

| # | Persona | Organization Type | Primary Value | Revenue Tier | Priority |
|---|---------|-------------------|---------------|--------------|----------|
| 1 | [Commodity Analyst](01_Commodity_Analyst.md) | Hedge fund / Asset manager | Research efficiency | Team/Enterprise | **P0** |
| 2 | [Portfolio Manager](02_Portfolio_Manager.md) | Investment fund | Decision speed | Enterprise | **P0** |
| 3 | [Mining Executive](03_Mining_Executive.md) | Mining company | Strategic intelligence | Enterprise | **P0** |
| 4 | [Commodity Trader](04_Commodity_Trader.md) | Trading house | Trading edge | Team/Enterprise | **P1** |
| 5 | [ESG Consultant](05_ESG_Consultant.md) | Consultancy | ESG data & compliance | Team | **P1** |
| 6 | [Investment Banker](06_Investment_Banker.md) | Investment bank | Deal intelligence | Enterprise | **P1** |
| 7 | [Government Official](07_Government_Official.md) | Government ministry | Policy intelligence | Pro/Team | **P2** |
| 8 | [Junior Mining CEO](08_Junior_Mining_CEO.md) | Junior explorer | Market intelligence | Pro | **P2** |

## Persona Distribution by Revenue

```
Enterprise (60% of revenue)
├── Portfolio Manager
├── Mining Executive
├── Investment Banker
└── Commodity Trader (Team/Enterprise)

Team (25% of revenue)
├── Commodity Analyst
├── ESG Consultant
└── Commodity Trader

Pro (15% of revenue)
├── Government Official
├── Junior Mining CEO
└── Commodity Analyst (individual)
```

## Cross-Persona Features
All personas benefit from:
- **Conversational AI**: Natural language queries across all data
- **Real-time alerts**: Customizable, agent-driven notifications
- **Mobile access**: Full functionality on iOS and Android
- **Data export**: CSV, Excel, PDF, API
- **Collaboration**: Share insights, annotations, and reports

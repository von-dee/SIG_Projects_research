## Background
Marcus runs a $1.2B natural resources book. He makes directional bets on commodities and mining equities. He relies on his analyst team for deep research but needs to form conviction quickly. He has used Wood Mackenzie for years but is frustrated by the lack of real-time integration with his trading systems.

## Daily Workflow
1. **5:30 AM**: Pre-market briefing, review positions
2. **7:00 AM**: Trade execution window
3. **9:00 AM**: Team standup, assign research priorities
4. **10:00 AM–4:00 PM**: Meetings, calls, reading, decision-making
5. **4:00 PM**: Position review, risk assessment

## Pain Points
- Cannot get instant answers during volatile market moments
- Research reports are too long — needs executive summaries with conviction levels
- No integration between research platform and portfolio management system
- ESG risks (labor strikes, environmental incidents) surface too late
- Difficult to stress-test portfolio against commodity price scenarios

## Goals
- Get instant, high-confidence answers during trading hours
- See real-time risk indicators for his mining equity positions
- Receive early warning signals for portfolio-level risks
- Generate scenario analyses on demand

## Technology Profile
- **Primary Tools**: Bloomberg Terminal, FactSet, Excel, internal PM system
- **Communication**: Bloomberg IB, phone, Slack
- **Mobile Usage**: Critical — trades and monitors on mobile
- **AI Comfort**: Medium — skeptical of AI hallucinations, wants citations

## Success Metrics
- Time to get conviction on a new thesis: < 30 minutes
- False positive rate on alerts: < 10%
- Portfolio risk visibility: Real-time
- Scenario analysis generation: < 5 minutes

## Quote
> "I don't have time to read a 40-page report. Tell me in two sentences whether I should buy or sell, and show me the data that backs it up."

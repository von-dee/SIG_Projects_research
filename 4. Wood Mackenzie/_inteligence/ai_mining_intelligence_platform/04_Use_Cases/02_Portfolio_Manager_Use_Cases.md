# Use Cases: Portfolio Manager (Marcus Okafor)

## UC-PM-001: Instant Market Intelligence During Volatility
**Trigger**: Copper drops 8% in 2 hours. Marcus needs to know why and whether to cut positions.
**Flow**:
1. Marcus opens Minerva mobile app and asks: *"Why is copper down 8% today?"*
2. AI agent correlates price movement with news, order flow, macro data, and sentiment.
3. Response: *"Copper down 8% due to: (1) Chinese property data miss (-12% YoY), (2) LME inventory build +45Kt, (3) USD strength (+1.2%). Probability of sustained decline: 62%."*
4. Marcus asks: *"Which positions are most exposed?"*
5. Platform shows portfolio heat map with position-level P&L impact and correlation analysis.

**Success Criteria**: Root cause identified in < 2 minutes; portfolio impact quantified in < 5 minutes.

## UC-PM-002: Portfolio Risk Dashboard
**Trigger**: Daily morning risk review.
**Flow**:
1. Marcus opens the "Portfolio Risk" dashboard.
2. Real-time view shows: commodity exposure by metal, geographic concentration, ESG risk score, supply chain vulnerabilities.
3. AI-generated risk summary: *"Top 3 risks: (1) 18% exposure to Chilean assets — water stress elevated, (2) Lithium positions vulnerable to Chinese oversupply, (3) Gold hedge ratio below target."*
4. He clicks any risk for drill-down analysis and mitigation suggestions.

**Success Criteria**: Full portfolio risk assessment in < 5 minutes; actionable insights with confidence scores.

## UC-PM-003: Scenario Stress Testing
**Trigger**: Marcus wants to stress-test his portfolio against a China hard landing scenario.
**Flow**:
1. Marcus opens "Scenario Builder" and selects "China Hard Landing" template.
2. Platform shows pre-built assumptions: GDP -3%, property starts -30%, steel demand -15%, copper demand -8%.
3. He adjusts assumptions and clicks "Run."
4. Results show: portfolio P&L impact (-$45M), individual position impacts, correlation breakdown, hedging suggestions.
5. He exports the scenario report for the investment committee.

**Success Criteria**: Scenario analysis completed in < 10 minutes; includes hedging recommendations and probability-weighted outcomes.

## UC-PM-004: ESG Risk Monitoring
**Trigger**: A tailings dam incident is reported in Brazil.
**Flow**:
1. Minerva's ESG agent detects the incident and cross-references with Marcus's portfolio.
2. Alert: *"Tailings dam incident at Vale's Brucutu mine. Your portfolio has 2.3% exposure to Vale ADRs. Estimated production impact: 15%. ESG score downgrade likely."*
3. Marcus reviews the incident details, historical precedents, and likely market reaction.
4. He decides to trim the position and sets a stop-loss.

**Success Criteria**: Portfolio-relevant ESG alert delivered < 30 minutes from incident; includes position-level impact.

## UC-PM-005: Investment Thesis Validation
**Trigger**: Marcus is considering a new position in a lithium producer.
**Flow**:
1. Marcus asks: *"Should I buy Albemarle at current levels?"*
2. AI agent generates a comprehensive thesis: valuation metrics, cost position vs. peers, supply/demand outlook, ESG profile, management quality, catalyst calendar.
3. He toggles between bull/base/bear cases with probability weightings.
4. He asks follow-ups: *"What's the bear case for lithium prices?"* or *"How does Albemarle's cost curve position compare to SQM?"*
5. He exports the thesis as a one-page summary for his investment memo.

**Success Criteria**: Full investment thesis generated in < 15 minutes; includes bull/base/bear scenarios with citations.

## UC-PM-006: Conviction Summary for Morning Meeting
**Trigger**: Marcus needs a 2-minute briefing for the 8:30 AM investment committee.
**Flow**:
1. Marcus opens "Morning Brief" at 8:25 AM.
2. AI-generated summary: overnight market moves, key news, portfolio impact, today's catalysts, recommended actions.
3. Each point includes a "drill down" link for detail.
4. He presents directly from the app or exports to one slide.

**Success Criteria**: Briefing generated automatically at market open; covers all portfolio-relevant events in < 2 minutes reading time.

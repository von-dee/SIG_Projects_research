# Use Cases: Mining Executive (Elena Vasquez)

## UC-EX-001: Real-Time Cost Benchmarking
**Trigger**: Elena needs to present cost position to the board next week.
**Flow**:
1. Elena opens "Cost Benchmarking" and selects her company's 5 copper assets.
2. Platform shows her assets on the global C1 cost curve, percentile rankings, and trend over 5 years.
3. She compares against specific peers: BHP, Freeport, Antofagasta.
4. She identifies her flagship asset is at the 65th percentile — above the peer median.
5. She drills into cost drivers: energy (32%), labor (28%), reagents (15%).
6. She exports the analysis as a board-ready presentation.

**Success Criteria**: Benchmarking analysis completed in < 30 minutes; board-ready output with peer comparisons.

## UC-EX-002: Competitor Production Monitoring
**Trigger**: Elena wants to track what her competitors are producing and at what cost.
**Flow**:
1. Elena sets up a "Competitor Watchlist" for 15 peer companies.
2. Minerva monitors quarterly reports, production updates, and news for each.
3. Weekly digest: production changes, cost guidance revisions, capex updates, strategic announcements.
4. She receives an alert: *"Freeport revised 2026 copper guidance down 5% due to Grasberg geotechnical issues."*
5. She assesses competitive implications for her own production planning.

**Success Criteria**: Competitor intelligence updated within 24 hours of public disclosure; includes impact assessment.

## UC-EX-003: M&A Target Identification
**Trigger**: Elena's CEO asks for acquisition targets in the lithium space.
**Flow**:
1. Elena opens "M&A Screener" and sets filters: lithium, pre-production, < $500M EV, North America, JORC resource > 2Mt LCE.
2. Platform returns 12 targets with full profiles: asset data, management, funding status, comparable transactions, risk assessment.
3. She ranks by strategic fit score and selects 3 for deep dive.
4. For each target, she views: NPV sensitivity, peer valuation multiples, ESG profile, geopolitical risk.
5. She exports a target summary for the M&A committee.

**Success Criteria**: Initial screen completed in < 1 hour; deep-dive data available for shortlisted targets.

## UC-EX-004: Board Presentation Generation
**Trigger**: Quarterly board meeting in 3 days.
**Flow**:
1. Elena opens "Board Pack Generator" and selects template: "Q2 2026 Strategic Update."
2. Platform auto-populates: market overview, company performance vs. peers, cost trends, ESG highlights, risk register, outlook.
3. She reviews each section, adds commentary, and adjusts emphasis.
4. She generates the full pack: 25 slides with charts, tables, and AI-generated speaker notes.
5. She exports to PowerPoint and shares with the board portal.

**Success Criteria**: First draft generated in < 2 hours; publication-ready in < 1 day vs. 2 weeks manually.

## UC-EX-005: ESG Compliance Dashboard
**Trigger**: ESG rating agency (MSCI) has flagged water usage concerns.
**Flow**:
1. Elena opens the "ESG Dashboard" for her company's assets.
2. View shows: water usage intensity vs. peers, recycling rates, community water agreements, regulatory compliance status.
3. AI-generated gap analysis: *"Your water intensity is 15% above peer median. Key improvement areas: (1) tailings water recycling, (2) desalination integration, (3) community engagement."*
4. She assigns action items to operations teams with deadlines.
5. She tracks progress and updates the board monthly.

**Success Criteria**: ESG gap analysis completed in < 1 hour; action plan with measurable KPIs generated automatically.

## UC-EX-006: Capital Allocation Scenario Modeling
**Trigger**: Elena needs to recommend how to allocate $500M capex budget.
**Flow**:
1. Elena opens "Capital Allocator" and inputs 5 project options with estimated NPVs, risks, and strategic fit scores.
2. Platform runs Monte Carlo simulations across commodity price scenarios.
3. Results show: expected value, downside risk, and probability of success for each allocation strategy.
4. She tests different combinations: all-in on brownfield expansion vs. diversification into new metals.
5. She exports the analysis with recommendation and risk disclosure for the board.

**Success Criteria**: Scenario modeling completed in < 2 hours; includes probabilistic outcomes and risk-adjusted recommendations.

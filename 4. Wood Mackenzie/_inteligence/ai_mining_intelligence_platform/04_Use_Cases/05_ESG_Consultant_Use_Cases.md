# Use Cases: ESG Consultant (Priya Sharma)

## UC-ES-001: ESG Profile Generation for New Client
**Trigger**: Priya needs to assess the ESG profile of a new mining client with 12 assets.
**Flow**:
1. Priya opens "ESG Profiler" and inputs the client's asset list.
2. Platform auto-generates profiles for each asset: environmental (water, emissions, biodiversity), social (community relations, labor practices, safety), governance (transparency, board structure, anti-corruption).
3. Each profile includes: current status, peer comparison, regulatory gaps, improvement recommendations.
4. Priya reviews, adds her own assessments, and generates a consolidated client report.
5. She exports to PDF for the client presentation.

**Success Criteria**: 12-asset ESG profile generated in < 4 hours vs. 2 weeks manually; includes peer benchmarking and gap analysis.

## UC-ES-002: Real-Time ESG Incident Monitoring
**Trigger**: Priya needs to monitor ESG incidents across her client portfolio.
**Flow**:
1. Priya sets up an "ESG Watch" for 25 client assets across 8 countries.
2. Minerva's ESG agent monitors: news, NGO reports, regulatory filings, social media, satellite imagery.
3. Alert: *"Tailings dam concern raised at Client X's Peruvian asset by local NGO. Source: Earthworks report. Severity: Medium. Recommended action: Request independent audit."*
4. Priya reviews the source material, assesses credibility, and alerts the client.
5. She tracks the incident through resolution.

**Success Criteria**: ESG incident alert delivered < 1 hour from public reporting; includes severity assessment and recommended action.

## UC-ES-003: Regulatory Compliance Tracking
**Trigger**: EU Taxonomy and SEC climate disclosure rules are expanding.
**Flow**:
1. Priya opens "Regulatory Tracker" and selects jurisdictions: EU, US, Canada, Australia.
2. Platform shows: current regulations, upcoming deadlines, reporting requirements, and her clients' compliance status.
3. AI-generated gap analysis for each client: *"Client Y is 60% compliant with EU Taxonomy. Missing: (1) Scope 3 emissions data, (2) biodiversity impact assessment, (3) circular economy metrics."*
4. Priya assigns tasks to her team and tracks progress.
5. She generates compliance reports for regulatory submission.

**Success Criteria**: Multi-jurisdiction compliance status tracked in real time; gap analysis with action items generated automatically.

## UC-ES-004: ESG Benchmarking Report
**Trigger**: Client wants to know how they rank against peers on ESG metrics.
**Flow**:
1. Priya opens "ESG Benchmarking" and selects the client's industry (copper mining) and peer group (10 companies).
2. Platform generates comparison across 50+ ESG KPIs: emissions intensity, water recycling, safety incidents, community investment, board diversity.
3. Client's position highlighted: top quartile on safety, bottom quartile on water efficiency.
4. AI-generated narrative explains rankings and suggests improvement targets.
5. Priya exports as a board-ready report with charts and tables.

**Success Criteria**: Peer benchmarking report generated in < 1 day; covers 50+ KPIs with improvement recommendations.

## UC-ES-005: Tailings Dam Risk Assessment
**Trigger**: Client's investor is asking about tailings dam safety across the portfolio.
**Flow**:
1. Priya opens "Tailings Risk" module and inputs client's 8 tailings facilities.
2. Platform shows: dam type, construction method, independent audit status, seismic risk, downstream population, historical incidents.
3. AI-generated risk matrix: 2 facilities rated "High Risk" — both upstream construction, no independent review in 3+ years.
4. Priya generates a remediation plan with cost estimates and timelines.
5. She presents to the client and investor.

**Success Criteria**: Tailings risk assessment completed in < 2 hours; includes quantified risk scores and remediation plans.

# Use Cases: Investment Banker (David Kim)

## UC-IB-001: Pitch Book Data Pack Generation
**Trigger**: David is pitching M&A advisory to a copper producer.
**Flow**:
1. David opens "Pitch Builder" and selects "M&A Advisory — Copper Sector."
2. Platform auto-generates: market overview, comparable transactions, valuation multiples, strategic rationale, buyer universe, timeline.
3. He customizes with client-specific data and recent market developments.
4. He adds his firm's credentials and case studies.
5. He exports to PowerPoint — 40 slides generated in 3 hours vs. 2 weeks manually.

**Success Criteria**: Pitch book first draft generated in < 4 hours; includes market data, comps, and strategic analysis.

---

## UC-IB-002: Comparable Company Analysis
**Trigger**: David needs to value a target mining company for a fairness opinion.
**Flow**:
1. David opens "Comps Builder" and inputs the target's details: copper producer, 150Ktpa, C1 costs $2.50/lb, reserves 5Mt.
2. Platform identifies 15 comparable companies with auto-calculated multiples: EV/Reserves, EV/Production, P/NAV, EV/EBITDA.
3. He adjusts the peer set, adds premium/discount factors, and runs sensitivity analysis.
4. Platform generates a valuation range with probability distribution.
5. He exports the analysis for the fairness opinion memo.

**Success Criteria**: Comparable company analysis completed in < 2 hours; includes 15+ peers with multiple valuation methodologies.

## UC-IB-003: M&A Pipeline Tracking
**Trigger**: David wants to know which assets are "in play" for acquisition.
**Flow**:
1. David opens "M&A Pipeline" and selects sector: lithium.
2. Platform shows: rumored deals, announced transactions, assets with strategic review flags, companies with activist investors.
3. AI-generated probability scores for each rumored deal.
4. He sets alerts for new rumors or status changes on tracked deals.
5. He shares intelligence with his deal team via workspace integration.

**Success Criteria**: Pipeline updated daily; deal probability scores with supporting evidence; alert on new developments < 1 hour.

## UC-IB-004: Due Diligence Data Room Enhancement
**Trigger**: David is running DD on a nickel acquisition target.
**Flow**:
1. David opens "DD Intelligence" and inputs the target company.
2. Platform auto-populates: asset-level production history, cost trends, reserve estimates, ESG incidents, management background, legal disputes, peer comparisons.
3. He identifies red flags: *"Reserve estimate downgraded by 20% in 2024. Independent review recommended."*
4. He generates a DD checklist with Minerva data pre-filled.
5. He shares with his DD team and tracks completion.

**Success Criteria**: DD intelligence pack generated in < 1 day; includes red flag identification and pre-populated checklists.

---

## UC-IB-005: Valuation Model Building
**Trigger**: David needs to build a DCF model for a gold acquisition.
**Flow**:
1. David opens "Model Builder" and selects "Gold DCF — Acquisition."
2. Platform pre-populates: production profile, cost assumptions, capex schedule, commodity price forecasts, discount rate.
3. He adjusts assumptions based on DD findings.
4. Platform runs sensitivity analysis: NPV at $1,800/oz, $2,000/oz, $2,200/oz gold prices.
5. He exports the model to Excel with formulas intact.

**Success Criteria**: DCF model built in < 4 hours vs. 2 days manually; includes sensitivity analysis and exportable Excel.

## UC-IB-006: Investor Target Identification
**Trigger**: David's client (a junior miner) needs to raise $100M.
**Flow**:
1. David opens "Investor Screener" and sets filters: mining-focused funds, $500M+ AUM, recent lithium investments, North America presence.
2. Platform returns 25 potential investors with profiles: recent deals, investment thesis, contact info, relationship mapping.
3. He ranks by strategic fit and likelihood of interest.
4. He generates a target list with outreach strategy for each.

**Success Criteria**: Investor target list generated in < 2 hours; includes relationship mapping and outreach strategy.

# Use Cases: Government Official (Dr. Amara Osei)

## UC-GO-001: Fiscal Regime Benchmarking
**Trigger**: Dr. Osei needs to compare Ghana's mining tax regime with peer countries.
**Flow**:
1. Dr. Osei opens "Fiscal Benchmarking" and selects "Gold Mining — African Jurisdictions."
2. Platform shows: royalty rates, corporate tax, withholding tax, state equity participation, stability agreements for 15 African countries.
3. Ghana's position highlighted: 5% royalty (median), 35% corporate tax (above median), 10% state equity (above median).
4. AI-generated competitiveness score: 72/100 — attractive but not top-tier.
5. She exports the analysis for the minister's briefing.

**Success Criteria**: Fiscal benchmarking completed in < 2 hours; includes competitiveness scoring and policy recommendations.

## UC-GO-002: Investment Flow Tracking
**Trigger**: Dr. Osei needs to track FDI into mining across Africa.
**Flow**:
1. Dr. Osei opens "Investment Tracker" and selects "Mining FDI — Africa — 2020–2026."
2. Platform shows: deal flow by country, commodity, investor type, value, stage.
3. Trend analysis: *"Lithium FDI into Africa up 400% since 2022. Ghana has captured 3% of this flow vs. 12% for DRC and 8% for Zimbabwe."*
4. She identifies gaps in Ghana's investment attraction strategy.
5. She generates a report for the investment promotion agency.

**Success Criteria**: Investment flow analysis completed in < 1 day; includes trend identification and gap analysis.

---

## UC-GO-003: Policy Impact Modeling
**Trigger**: Ministry is considering a 2% royalty increase for gold.
**Flow**:
1. Dr. Osei opens "Policy Simulator" and inputs the proposed change: gold royalty from 5% to 7%.
2. Platform models: revenue impact (+$150M/year), investment attractiveness change (-8% score), competitor response likelihood, production impact (-3% over 5 years).
3. She tests scenarios: phased increase vs. immediate, combined with tax incentives.
4. She exports the analysis with recommendation for cabinet.

**Success Criteria**: Policy impact modeled in < 1 day; includes revenue, investment, and production effects with scenario comparison.

## UC-GO-004: International Reporting Automation
**Trigger**: IMF Article IV consultation requires mining sector data.
**Flow**:
1. Dr. Osei opens "Report Generator" and selects "IMF Article IV — Mining Sector."
2. Platform auto-populates: production data, export values, fiscal contributions, employment, reserves, investment pipeline.
3. She reviews, adds qualitative commentary, and generates the report.
4. She exports to Word/PDF for submission.

**Success Criteria**: IMF report generated in < 1 day vs. 2 weeks manually; data auto-populated from verified sources.

## UC-GO-005: Artisanal Mining Monitoring
**Trigger**: Dr. Osei needs to track artisanal and small-scale mining (ASM) activity.
**Flow**:
1. Dr. Osei opens "ASM Monitor" for Ghana's gold regions.
2. Platform shows: estimated ASM production, environmental impact zones, conflict incidents, formalization progress.
3. Satellite imagery comparison shows: 15% expansion in ASM activity in Western Region over 6 months.
4. She identifies hotspots for intervention and generates a policy brief.

**Success Criteria**: ASM monitoring updated monthly; includes satellite imagery, production estimates, and intervention recommendations.

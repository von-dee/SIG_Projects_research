# Use Cases: Junior Mining CEO (Tom Bradley)

## UC-JM-001: Market Timing for Capital Raise
**Trigger**: Tom needs to decide when to launch his next financing round.
**Flow**:
1. Tom opens "Market Timing" module for lithium.
2. Platform shows: lithium price trend, EV sales momentum, investor sentiment index, comparable company performance, recent financing activity.
3. AI-generated recommendation: *"Optimal financing window: next 4–6 weeks. Rationale: lithium price recovery (+15% in 30 days), strong EV sales data, 3 comparable financings in past month at premium valuations."*
4. Tom reviews the supporting data and sets a board meeting to approve the raise.

**Success Criteria**: Financing timing recommendation generated in < 10 minutes; includes multi-factor rationale and comparable transactions.

---

## UC-JM-002: Project Benchmarking Against Peers
**Trigger**: Tom needs to position his Nevada lithium project against comparable developments.
**Flow**:
1. Tom opens "Project Benchmarking" and inputs his project details: 3.2Mt LCE resource, PFS stage, $850M capex, 20Ktpa LCE production.
2. Platform identifies 12 comparable projects with full profiles: resource size, grade, capex, opex, timeline, funding status.
3. His project ranked: top quartile on resource size, median on capex intensity, below median on funding progress.
4. AI-generated positioning: *"Strong resource base but funding gap is a concern. Recommend: secure offtake agreement to de-risk for equity raise."*
5. He exports the benchmarking for investor presentations.

**Success Criteria**: Project benchmarking completed in < 1 hour; includes 12+ comparables with strategic recommendations.

## UC-JM-003: Investor Presentation Building
**Trigger**: Tom has a roadshow in 2 weeks and needs to update his deck.
**Flow**:
1. Tom opens "Deck Builder" and selects "Junior Mining — Lithium — Investor Presentation."
2. Platform auto-generates: market overview, project summary, economics, team, ESG narrative, comparable transactions, use of proceeds.
3. He uploads his geological maps and site photos.
4. He customizes the narrative and adds his vision statement.
5. He exports to PowerPoint — 25 slides in 4 hours vs. 2 weeks manually.

**Success Criteria**: Investor deck first draft generated in < 4 hours; includes market data, economics, and comparables.

## UC-JM-004: M&A Acquirer Tracking
**Trigger**: Tom wants to know which majors might acquire his company.
**Flow**:
1. Tom opens "Acquirer Tracker" and inputs his company profile.
2. Platform identifies 8 potential acquirers with rationale: strategic fit, recent M&A activity, balance sheet capacity, management statements.
3. He tracks each acquirer's activity: recent site visits, analyst commentary, capital allocation priorities.
4. Alert: *"Albemarle CEO mentioned 'North American lithium assets' as strategic priority in Q1 earnings call."*
5. He prepares outreach strategy for the most likely acquirers.

**Success Criteria**: Acquirer tracking updated weekly; includes strategic rationale and real-time alerts on relevant statements.

---

## UC-JM-005: ESG Narrative Building
**Trigger**: Tom's investors are asking for a comprehensive ESG story.
**Flow**:
1. Tom opens "ESG Narrative Builder" for his Nevada project.
2. Platform auto-generates: environmental baseline (water, land, biodiversity), social engagement plan, governance structure, community benefits.
3. He adds project-specific initiatives: local hiring targets, water recycling plan, Indigenous engagement.
4. Platform generates an ESG score and compares against peer projects.
5. He exports as a standalone ESG report for investor due diligence.

**Success Criteria**: ESG narrative generated in < 1 day; includes benchmarking and improvement roadmap.

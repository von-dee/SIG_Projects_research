# Go-to-Market Strategy

## Market Entry Strategy

### Beachhead Vertical: Lithium & Battery Metals
**Rationale**:
- Fastest-growing commodity segment (EV transition)
- Highest information asymmetry
- Most price volatility (opportunity for real-time intelligence)
- Fragmented data landscape (no dominant incumbent)
- Strong investor and corporate interest

**Target Accounts (First 12 Months)**:
- 5 lithium-focused hedge funds ($500M–$5B AUM)
- 3 lithium producers (Pilbara Minerals, Albemarle, SQM)
- 2 battery OEMs (CATL, LG Energy Solution)
- 3 commodity trading houses (Glencore, Trafigura, Mercuria)

## Customer Acquisition Strategy

### 1. Design Partner Program (Months 0–12)
**Objective**: Validate product-market fit with 5–10 engaged users
**Structure**:
- Free access to Pro tier for 6 months
- Weekly feedback sessions
- Co-development of features
- Case study and testimonial rights
- First right of refusal on annual contracts

**Target Design Partners**:
| Company Type | Target | Value |
|--------------|--------|-------|
| Hedge fund analyst | 2 | Validation of research workflows |
| Mining company strategist | 2 | Validation of benchmarking tools |
| Commodity trader | 1 | Validation of real-time alerts |
| ESG consultant | 1 | Validation of ESG features |
| Investment banker | 1 | Validation of deal intelligence |

### 2. Content Marketing & Thought Leadership
**Channels**:
- **Substack/Newsletter**: Weekly "Lithium Lens" — AI-generated market brief
- **Twitter/X**: Daily market insights, data visualizations
- **LinkedIn**: Long-form analysis, company updates
- **Blog**: Technical deep-dives, product updates
- **Podcast**: "The Lithium Ledger" — interviews with industry leaders

**Content Pillars**:
1. Market Intelligence (price forecasts, supply-demand)
2. Technology (AI in mining, data science)
3. ESG (sustainability, responsible sourcing)
4. Deal Flow (M&A, financing, project updates)
5. Product Education (how-to guides, feature spotlights)

### 3. Conference & Event Strategy
**Target Events (Year 1)**:
| Event | Timing | Objective |
|-------|--------|-----------|
| PDAC (Toronto) | March | Brand awareness, lead gen |
| LME Week (London) | October | Trading community engagement |
| Benchmark World Lithium | May | Vertical-specific positioning |
| CRU World Copper | April | Expansion signal |
| Diggers & Dealers (Australia) | August | APAC market entry |

**Tactics**:
- Speaking slots (founder/domain expert)
- Booth presence (Year 2)
- Private dinners with target accounts
- Demo stations for hands-on trials

### 4. Product-Led Growth (PLG)
**Freemium Strategy**:
- Free tier: 3-day delayed data, 10 queries/day, basic chat
- Pro tier: Real-time data, 500 queries/mo, basic alerts
- Team tier: Unlimited queries, advanced features, API access
- Enterprise: Full platform, custom integrations, dedicated support

**Viral Loops**:
- Shareable insights (one-click Twitter/LinkedIn sharing)
- Team invites (referral credits)
- Public dashboards (embeddable widgets)
- Report sharing (branded PDFs with Minerva watermark)

### 5. Outbound Sales
**Inside Sales** (Team tier):
- Target: Mid-size funds, trading desks, consultancies
- Motion: Demo → 2-week trial → annual contract
- Tools: Salesforce, Outreach, ZoomInfo

**Field Sales** (Enterprise tier):
- Target: Tier-1 miners, major funds, trading houses
- Motion: C-suite engagement → POC → 6-month pilot → annual contract
- Tools: Salesforce, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Gong

## Sales Motion

### Self-Serve (Pro Tier)
1. User discovers via content/search
2. Signs up for free tier
3. Hits usage limits → upgrade prompt
4. Credit card payment → instant activation
5. In-app onboarding + email nurture

### Inside Sales (Team Tier)
1. SDR qualifies lead via website behavior + firmographic data
2. Demo booking (30 min)
3. 2-week trial with dedicated onboarding specialist
4. Proposal + annual contract
5. Implementation + training

### Field Sales (Enterprise)
1. AE engages C-suite via warm intro or conference
2. Discovery call (understand pain points)
3. Custom demo + POC proposal
4. 6-month POC with success metrics
5. Business case + procurement
6. Annual contract with expansion clauses

## Competitive Positioning

### Positioning Statement
> "For commodity analysts and mining strategists who are frustrated with expensive, static research reports, Minerva is an AI-powered intelligence platform that delivers real-time, conversational, and personalized insights at a fraction of the cost of legacy providers."

### Key Messages
1. **Speed**: "Get answers in seconds, not days"
2. **Cost**: "10x cheaper than Wood Mackenzie"
3. **Interactivity**: "Talk to your data, don't read PDFs"
4. **Integration**: "Blend public intelligence with your private data"
5. **Proactivity**: "AI agents that watch the market so you don't have to"

## Account Penetration Strategy

### Year 1: Validation (5–10 accounts)
- Focus: Design partner program
- Target: Mix of Tier 1 and Tier 2
- Goal: Product-market fit validation, case studies, testimonials

### Year 2: Scale (50–100 accounts)
- Focus: Team tier expansion
- Target: Tier 2 mining companies, mid-size funds
- Goal: $3M ARR, proven sales motion

### Year 3: Enterprise (200–400 accounts)
- Focus: Enterprise tier penetration
- Target: Tier 1 miners, major funds, trading houses
- Goal: $12M ARR, 125% NRR

### Year 4+: Market Leadership (500–1000 accounts)
- Focus: Full market coverage
- Target: All tiers, all geographies
- Goal: $35M+ ARR, industry standard

# Partnership Opportunities

## Partnership Strategy Overview
Minerva's partnership strategy is designed to accelerate market entry, enhance data capabilities, and build credibility through association with established industry players. Partnerships fall into four categories:
1. **Data Partnerships**: Access to proprietary data sources
2. **Distribution Partnerships**: Channel sales and co-marketing
3. **Technology Partnerships**: Platform integrations and joint development
4. **Industry Partnerships**: Association memberships and thought leadership

### Phase 3: Scale (Months 12–18)

- [ ] Snowflake/Databricks marketplace listing
- [ ] 5+ distribution partnerships
- [ ] Consulting firm referral agreements
- [ ] Enterprise co-sell with cloud providers

### Phase 4: Market Leadership (Months 18–24)
- [ ] Bloomberg Terminal full integration
- [ ] Industry standard recognition
- [ ] Strategic acquisition discussions (data providers)
- [ ] International expansion partnerships

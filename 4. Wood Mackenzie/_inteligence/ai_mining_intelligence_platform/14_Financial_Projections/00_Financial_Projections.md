# Financial Projections

## Assumptions

### Revenue Model
- **SaaS Subscriptions**: 70% of revenue
- **API & Data Licensing**: 15% of revenue
- **Consulting & Advisory**: 10% of revenue
- **Partnership Revenue**: 5% of revenue

### Pricing Assumptions
| Tier | Monthly Price | Annual Price (with 20% discount) |
|------|--------------|----------------------------------|
| Pro | $499 | $4,790 |
| Team | $2,499 | $23,990 |
| Enterprise | $15,000–$50,000 | $144,000–$480,000 |

### Customer Mix Assumptions
| Year | Pro | Team | Enterprise | Total |
|------|-----|------|------------|-------|
| 1 | 10 | 4 | 1 | 15 |
| 2 | 50 | 20 | 10 | 80 |
| 3 | 150 | 60 | 40 | 250 |
| 4 | 300 | 150 | 150 | 600 |
| 5 | 500 | 300 | 400 | 1,200 |

### Growth Assumptions
- **Customer Growth**: 5x Year 1→2, 3x Year 2→3, 2.4x Year 3→4, 2x Year 4→5
- **Net Revenue Retention**: 110% (Year 2), 125% (Year 3+), driven by upsells and expansions
- **Gross Margin**: 75% (Year 1) → 80% (Year 5), improving with scale
- **Churn Rate**: 5% monthly (Year 1) → 2% monthly (Year 5)

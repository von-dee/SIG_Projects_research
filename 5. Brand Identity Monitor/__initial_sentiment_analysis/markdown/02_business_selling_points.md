# Business Selling Points

## Positioning

BrandWatch Africa with sentiment intelligence is a reputation early-warning system for companies operating in high-trust, high-scrutiny environments. It helps organizations understand what people feel, why they feel it, where the narrative is spreading, and what action should happen next.

## Core Promise

Move from:

> "We saw mentions of our brand."

To:

> "We know which communities are losing trust, which issues are driving sentiment, and what response is needed before the problem escalates."

## Buyer Pain Points

### Community Relations Teams

- They hear about issues after they are already public.
- Field reports are manual, delayed, and hard to compare.
- They cannot easily prove whether engagement work is improving trust.
- They need early warning before protests, media attention, or regulator involvement.

### ESG And Sustainability Leaders

- Investor and board reports need measurable social-license indicators.
- Existing reports rely heavily on anecdotes and lagging survey data.
- ESG teams need evidence for GRI, IFC, and internal sustainability reporting.
- They need to show that community risk is being monitored continuously.

### Communications Teams

- Negative narratives spread across radio, social media, and local news before national coverage appears.
- Current tools miss local-language nuance and offline influence.
- Crisis response teams need prioritization, not more alerts.
- Media sentiment needs to be connected to stakeholder impact.

### Executives

- Brand reputation directly affects licensing, operations, investor confidence, and government relations.
- They need a single reputation risk view across sites, countries, and subsidiaries.
- They want to benchmark performance against competitors and industry averages.

## Differentiated Selling Points

### 1. Built For African Context

Unlike generic global tools, the platform is designed for African languages, community communication patterns, radio influence, and local stakeholder dynamics.

### 2. Community Risk, Not Vanity Metrics

The product does not stop at likes, mentions, or reach. It turns sentiment into operational risk signals that executives can act on.

### 3. ESG-Ready Evidence

Sentiment trends can be used in ESG reporting, board packs, sustainability reviews, and investor updates.

### 4. Early Warning Before Crisis

The system detects sentiment shifts before they become protests, shutdowns, viral campaigns, or regulatory attention.

### 5. Human-In-The-Loop Trust

Low-confidence or culturally ambiguous sentiment is flagged for review, making the AI more credible for serious business decisions.

### 6. Competitive Benchmarking

Companies can compare their sentiment, issue volume, and trust trajectory against peers in mining, telecom, banking, energy, FMCG, and government.

## Sales Messaging

### One-Line Pitch

An AI-powered sentiment intelligence platform that helps African businesses detect reputation risk before it becomes operational damage.

### Short Pitch

BrandWatch Africa analyzes social media, local news, radio transcripts, and community reports to reveal how people feel about your brand, which issues are driving perception, and what action your team should take next.

### Enterprise Pitch

For mining, energy, telecom, banking, and regulated industries, BrandWatch Africa provides an ESG-aligned reputation intelligence system that quantifies community sentiment, detects emerging risk, and supports defensible executive reporting across African markets.

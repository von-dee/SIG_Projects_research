# Business Model

## Revenue Strategy

The sentiment analysis extension should be packaged as a premium intelligence capability. Customers should pay for the value of risk reduction, ESG evidence, and decision support rather than raw mention volume alone.

## Pricing Packages

### Starter

Designed for small brands, agencies, and pilot customers.

Includes:

- 1 brand
- Limited source monitoring
- Basic sentiment dashboard
- Weekly sentiment summary
- CSV upload for field reports
- Email alerts

Suggested price:

- USD 99-299 per month

### Growth

Designed for mid-market companies and regional teams.

Includes:

- Multiple brands or projects
- Social, news, and uploaded reports
- Topic-level sentiment
- Emotion and intent detection
- Risk scoring
- Competitor benchmarking
- Monthly PDF reports
- Team collaboration

Suggested price:

- USD 499-1,500 per month

### Enterprise

Designed for mining companies, telecoms, banks, energy companies, and government agencies.

Includes:

- Multi-site and multi-country monitoring
- Radio transcript integration
- Local-language model tuning
- ESG reporting templates
- Custom risk scoring
- API access
- Dedicated onboarding
- Human review workflow
- SLA-backed alerts
- Executive dashboards

Suggested price:

- USD 2,500-10,000+ per month, depending on source volume and customization.

## Add-On Revenue

Potential add-ons:

- Radio monitoring package
- Local-language tuning package
- ESG report automation
- Crisis war-room dashboard
- Competitor intelligence module
- Historical data backfill
- Custom executive board reports
- API data access
- Managed analyst service

## Services Revenue

Services can accelerate adoption and create high-margin revenue.

Options:

- Setup and onboarding fee
- Custom taxonomy design
- Stakeholder mapping workshops
- Sentiment model calibration
- Quarterly reputation intelligence review
- Crisis-response simulation
- ESG reporting support

## Go-To-Market

### Beachhead Market

Start with mining and extractives in Ghana because the pain is urgent, budgets exist, and the current research already identifies strong gaps around community sentiment, ESG reporting, and social license to operate.

### Expansion Markets

After mining:

- Telecommunications
- Energy and utilities
- Banking and fintech
- FMCG
- Public sector and regulators
- Pan-African PR and communications agencies

## Sales Motion

Recommended sales motion:

- Consultative B2B sales for enterprise customers.
- Pilot-first adoption with one site, region, or brand.
- Convert pilots into annual contracts using measurable sentiment and risk insights.
- Use quarterly business reviews to upsell additional sites and modules.

## Pilot Offer

Suggested pilot:

- 8 to 12 weeks
- 1 brand or operating site
- Social, news, and uploaded community reports
- Weekly sentiment insights
- Final executive report
- Success review tied to crisis detection, stakeholder insight, or reporting time saved

## Key Buying Triggers

Customers are most likely to buy when:

- A community issue has recently escalated.
- Investor or board reporting requires stronger ESG evidence.
- A company is entering a new region or launching a sensitive project.
- A regulator or media outlet is increasing scrutiny.
- Customer complaints are rising faster than teams can triage them.
- Existing tools miss local-language or offline sentiment.

# Implementation Roadmap

## Phase 1: Sentiment MVP

Goal: Prove that sentiment analysis can turn mentions and reports into useful business signals.

Build:

- Mention ingestion from CSV uploads, news feeds, and selected social sources.
- Basic language detection.
- Positive, negative, neutral, mixed, and uncertain labels.
- Confidence scoring.
- Topic tags for common mining and brand issues.
- Basic dashboard with sentiment trend, source breakdown, and issue table.
- Manual review workflow for low-confidence results.

Success criteria:

- Users can upload or ingest mentions and see useful sentiment output.
- Human reviewers agree with model sentiment at an acceptable baseline.
- Teams can identify top negative topics without manual spreadsheet work.

## Phase 2: Context-Aware Sentiment

Goal: Improve accuracy and relevance for African market realities.

Build:

- Local-language examples and fine-tuning dataset.
- Code-switching support.
- Emotion and intent detection.
- Explainable sentiment output.
- Stakeholder group classification.
- Location extraction.
- Sentiment by community, region, source, and stakeholder.

Success criteria:

- Sentiment explanations are useful to non-technical users.
- The model handles common Ghanaian and African business expressions better than generic sentiment APIs.
- Users can see why sentiment is changing, not just that it changed.

## Phase 3: Risk And Alerts

Goal: Turn sentiment into prioritization and action.

Build:

- Community risk scoring.
- Alert thresholds by topic, channel, and location.
- Narrative velocity tracking.
- Suggested response owner and action.
- Escalation workflow for high-risk issues.
- Slack, email, SMS, or WhatsApp Business alerts where appropriate.

Success criteria:

- Users receive fewer but more useful alerts.
- High-risk issues are detected earlier than manual monitoring.
- Teams can track whether an issue was reviewed, assigned, and resolved.

## Phase 4: ESG And Executive Reporting

Goal: Make sentiment intelligence usable for formal reporting and leadership decisions.

Build:

- ESG-aligned report templates.
- Social license trend score.
- Community trust index.
- Board-ready PDF exports.
- Competitor benchmarking.
- Intervention impact analysis.
- Data export API.

Success criteria:

- ESG and executive teams use reports in monthly or quarterly reviews.
- Reporting time is reduced.
- Customers can connect sentiment trends to stakeholder engagement actions.

## Phase 5: Enterprise Intelligence Platform

Goal: Scale into a defensible enterprise reputation intelligence system.

Build:

- Multi-country support.
- Custom model tuning per customer.
- Radio and audio transcription integration.
- Advanced narrative clustering.
- Bot and synthetic content detection.
- Predictive crisis forecasting.
- Enterprise permissions and audit logs.
- Managed analyst workflows.

Success criteria:

- Enterprise customers expand from one site or team to multiple business units.
- Model performance improves through customer-specific feedback.
- The platform becomes a core reputation and ESG operating system.

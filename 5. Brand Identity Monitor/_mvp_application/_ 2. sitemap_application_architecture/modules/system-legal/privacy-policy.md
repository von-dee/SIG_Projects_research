# Privacy Policy

## Page Title & Description
Privacy Policy explains how BrandWatch Africa collects, processes, stores, protects, and shares personal data, community submissions, uploaded reports, source metadata, and analytics outputs.

## User Roles & Access Control
- Public guests and authenticated users can view this page.
- Admins can access organization-specific data processing addendum links from compliance settings.
- Legal text is read-only in the application.

## UI/UX Component Breakdown
- Policy content sections for data collected, use of data, sources, legal bases, retention, subprocessors, international transfers, user rights, contact, and updates.
- Table of contents with anchor links.
- Effective date and version history link.
- Download PDF button.
- Contact privacy team link.

## User Actions & Interactions
- Clicking anchors scrolls to sections.
- Downloading PDF retrieves current policy artifact.
- Contact link opens privacy inquiry form or email.
- Version history shows prior effective policies.

## Data Requirements & State
- Fetch current published policy version, effective date, PDF artifact, and prior version metadata.
- No tenant data is required for public view.
- Capture policy download analytics without collecting unnecessary personal data.

## Navigation & Routing
- Links to `/legal/terms`, `/legal/data-processing`, `/auth/register`, and `/settings/compliance`.
- Missing policy artifact routes to support fallback.
- Public route remains accessible during logged-out sessions.

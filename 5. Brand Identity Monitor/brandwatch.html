01 — THE CORE PROBLEM

The Disconnect

Global tools monitor 25M+ online sources, but fundamentally miss how African communities actually communicate.

Why Existing Solutions Fail

Radio is Primary

Ghana has 500+ licensed stations. Call-in shows are the primary forum for grievance expression. Global tools: Not available.

Language Barriers

Global tools miss Twi, Ga, Ewe, Dagbani. They fail to interpret sarcasm, proverbs, and culturally specific expressions.

Offline Discourse Missed

Town hall meetings, chief's courts, and market gossip are completely invisible to social listening tools.

The Stakes are Higher

  • Social License to Operate (SLO)

    Without community buy-in, operations face protests, blockades, and regulatory shutdowns.

  • ESG Investor Scrutiny

    Lenders now build community engagement requirements into loan covenants.

  • Regulatory Pressure

    Ghana's Minerals Commission is intensifying enforcement of local content regulations.

07 — RISK FACTORS

Mitigations & Moorings

Risk: Limited training data, tonal variations, and code-switching make accurate NLP challenging.

Mitigation: Partner with KNUST & UG linguistics, crowdsource via reporters, use transfer learning, and implement human-in-the-loop validation for 12 months.

Risk: Poor signal quality, power outages, and equipment failure in remote mining areas.

Mitigation: Redundant capture hardware (2 tuners/location), solar-powered off-grid equipment, direct audio feed partnerships, and 20% station overlap.

Risk: Mining companies may not see value in AI monitoring, leading to longer sales cycles.

Mitigation: Extensive onboarding/training, ROI calculators (cost of conflict vs. monitoring), 30-day free trials, and leveraging ESG compliance as an urgent sales driver.

Risk: Monitoring community conversations raises privacy and regulatory concerns under Ghana's Data Protection Act.

Mitigation: Monitor only public sources (radio, public social media), strict retention policies, privacy-by-design architecture, and transparency with communities.

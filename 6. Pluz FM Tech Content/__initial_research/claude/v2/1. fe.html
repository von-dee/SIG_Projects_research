How these companies make money

Broadcast spot advertising

Traditional 30/60-second ads sold to local and national advertisers. Still the largest single revenue source (~$10B market-wide), though down slightly year-over-year.

Flat YoY

Digital & programmatic audio

Ads delivered via streaming apps, online radio, and podcast feeds. Programmatic audio reached $2.3B in 2025 — up 18% — attracting digital-native advertisers at scale.

+18% YoY

Podcast advertising

Host-read, dynamically inserted, and programmatic podcast ads. iHeartMedia podcast revenue hit $174M in Q4 2025 alone, up 24% YoY. SiriusXM podcast revenue surged ~50% in Q3 2025.

+24% YoY

Subscription revenue

SiriusXM's core model: paid satellite and streaming subscriptions. Pandora Premium and ad-free tiers. Combined subscriber base drives the bulk of ~$8.7B annual revenue.

Stable

Events & sponsorships

iHeartRadio Music Festival, Jingle Ball, iHeart awards shows. Live events create premium sponsorship inventory and extend brand relationships beyond broadcast.

Growing

Digital marketing services

Townsquare and Cumulus both run significant digital agency arms — local SEO, social advertising, display — often earning more per market than radio spot revenue alone.

+12% YoY

Traffic & data licensing

iHeartMedia's Katz Media Group and RCS syndication arm license broadcast traffic data and ratings tools to smaller stations. RCS software products are sold globally as SaaS.

Stable

Political advertising

High-cyclical revenue in even-numbered years. 2024 was a presidential year — companies saw large spikes. 2025 saw declines excluding political compared to 2024 levels.

Off-cycle

Content syndication

Cumulus's Westwood One syndicates sports, news, and talk content (NCAA, NFL, Super Bowl) to 500+ affiliated stations, SiriusXM, and streaming platforms — earning rights fees.

Stable

Software categories & products in use

📻 Broadcast automation / playout

RCS Zetta & ZettaCloud

The dominant broadcast automation platform — handles playout sequencing, live assists, voice tracking, splits, and cloud-native radio for multi-station groups.

Used by: Major networks, iHeart, Audacy affiliates
📻 Broadcast automation / playout

WideOrbit Aurora

Full-stack automation for radio including centralized playlists, content ingest, music conversion, and market-wide distribution. Integrates with vCreative Cloud AutoDub.

Used by: Large group operators, syndication networks
🎵 Music scheduling

RCS GSelector / SelectorCloud

AI-powered music scheduling with rule-based rotation, format clocks, and audience targeting. SelectorCloud adds cloud-native AI scheduling for modern music workflows.

Used by: Commercial FM stations across all major groups
🎵 Music scheduling

PlayoutONE / Myriad

Mid-market automation and scheduling tools for independent and community stations. Known for ease of use, voice tracking, and flexible multi-format support.

Used by: Independent and community stations
📊 Traffic & billing

RCS Aquira

Traffic, billing, and order management system tightly integrated with Zetta and GSelector. Manages ad orders, reconciliation, and invoicing across multi-market operations.

Used by: RCS network stations
📊 Traffic & billing

WideOrbit Traffic

Broadcast traffic management with workflow automation for spot management, affidavits, and political ad compliance. Widely deployed across commercial radio.

Used by: Most top-40 commercial radio groups
💰 Ad tech & programmatic

AdsWizz (SiriusXM subsidiary)

The engine behind SiriusXM/Pandora ad monetization. Also powers third-party audio publishers. Provides DSP/SSP connectivity, programmatic buying, contextual targeting (60% of all global targeting on the platform).

Used by: SiriusXM, Pandora, 3rd-party publishers globally
💰 Ad tech & programmatic

Triton Digital / Omny Studio

Podcast hosting, measurement, and ad monetization. Triton's AdBuilder AI generates ads in 60+ languages. Partnered with Washington Post and hundreds of broadcasters for full podcast stack.

Used by: Washington Post, major podcast networks, broadcasters
🎙 Production & content

Adobe Audition

Industry-standard audio editing and production. Used for spot production, voice tracking, podcast mastering, and editing live recordings. Integrates with automation via Genesys.

Used by: Station production teams industry-wide
🎙 Production & content

ENCO aiTrack

AI-powered dynamic content insertion for playout. Browser-accessible; generates broadcast-ready audio in seconds using generative AI. Major evolution announced NAB 2026.

Used by: Forward-looking broadcasters adopting AI workflows
📡 Streaming & distribution

Simplecast (SiriusXM)

Podcast hosting and distribution platform owned by SiriusXM. Provides RSS distribution, analytics, and listener data used for targeted ad sales across the SiriusXM Media ecosystem.

Used by: SiriusXM podcast creators and partners
📡 Streaming & distribution

Triton Digital Streaming

Encoder, CDN, and stream management for online radio. Provides listener measurement (Webcast Metrics), competitive rankings, and stream monetization infrastructure.

Used by: Commercial and public radio stations
🔗 Identity & data

LiveRamp / Unified ID 2.0

Cookieless identity resolution for targeting and measurement. Pandora became the first audio publisher on UID2. SiriusXM and AdsWizz expanded LiveRamp integration in 2026 for cross-platform audience matching.

Used by: SiriusXM Media / Pandora for programmatic targeting
🔗 Identity & data

Amazon DSP / Yahoo DSP / Magnite

Demand-side platforms integrated with broadcast radio inventory. iHeartMedia and SiriusXM both made major moves in 2025 to make broadcast radio buyable programmatically through these DSPs.

Used by: iHeartMedia, SiriusXM, Audacy for programmatic sales
⚙️ Workflow & production ops

vCreative Cloud AutoDub

Automates audio spot production and delivery. Checks, processes, and delivers spots with correct format, metadata, and companion files. Integrates live with WideOrbit Aurora and RCS ZettaCloud.

Used by: Multi-market operators on WideOrbit or Zetta

Technology trends reshaping the industry

Cloud-native broadcasting

On-prem to cloud shift

RCS is migrating ZettaCloud from disaster recovery to full primary radio automation. 2026 is being called "the year of the cloud" in broadcast. Reduces hardware dependency and enables remote operations.

Accelerating
AI in every layer

AI content & scheduling

RCS SelectorCloud uses AI for music scheduling. ENCO aiTrack uses generative AI for content insertion. Triton's AdBuilder AI writes ads in 60+ languages. AI is now embedded in playout, scheduling, production, and ad creation.

Rapid growth
Programmatic radio

Broadcast to digital buying

iHeartMedia is building ad tech to make broadcast radio buyable via Amazon DSP, Yahoo DSP, and StackAdapt — the same way digital inventory is transacted. A structural shift in how radio is sold.

Emerging
Podcast video expansion

Audio to video podcast

Triton's Omny Studio added video podcast support in Dec 2025. Audioboom reports video now generates 13%+ of revenue. iHeartMedia launched a TikTok Podcast Network. The medium is converging with video.

Converging
Privacy-first targeting

Cookieless identity

Pandora became the first audio publisher on UID2. SiriusXM is using clean room technology with Amazon and LiveRamp to maintain targeting precision without third-party cookies.

Critical
Connected car & smart speakers

New distribution surfaces

Connected-car dashboards are delivering synchronized banner-plus-spot formats. Smart speaker adoption adds ~2.1% to radio's forecast CAGR. These surfaces create new programmatic inventory.

## **About WideOrbit**

WideOrbit is a **San Francisco-based ad tech platform** (founded 1999, now owned by Lumine Group/Constellation Software) that provides end-to-end software solutions for media companies to manage and automate advertising operations. It handles **~$37 billion in advertising dollars annually** across 6,450+ stations and networks. Its flagship products include WO Traffic (traffic management), WO Automation for Radio, and WO Network for network trafficking. The platform serves as the "operating system" for the sell-side of premium advertising — from pitch to payment — across linear TV, radio, and digital media.

## **Why These Companies Trust WideOrbit**

WideOrbit serves as the **critical infrastructure layer** that ties all these disparate business models together:

1. **Unified Ad Management:** From pitch to payment across linear TV, radio, and digital
2. **Traffic & Billing:** WO Traffic manages 90%+ of U.S. local TV ad revenue
3. **Programmatic Integration:** Connects traditional broadcast inventory to digital ad buyers
4. **Scale:** Processes $37B+ in ad dollars across 6,450+ stations
5. **Cross-Platform:** Handles the complexity of campaigns spanning broadcast + digital + streaming

The diversity of WideOrbit's client base — from local radio stations to global conglomerates — demonstrates how fundamental its platform is to the entire media advertising ecosystem.

Based on the HTML testimonials provided, here are the 8 unique companies featured, along with an analysis of what they do and their business models. 

*Note: All of these companies are clients of **Triton Digital**, an audio streaming and advertising technology provider. Their business models heavily revolve around digital audio, programmatic advertising, and audience data monetization.*

### 1. Christian Broadcasting System (CBS)
*   **Key Contact:** Eugine Park, Director of Digital Contents Department
*   **What They Do:** Broadcasting and digital content distribution.
*   **Business Model:** They focus on expanding their audience reach and **maximizing digital advertising revenue**. They achieve this by using robust technology solutions to easily manage and sell their digital advertising inventory alongside their traditional broadcast content.

### 2. Salem Media Group
*   **Key Contact:** David Santrella, CEO
*   **What They Do:** A major media and radio broadcasting group.
*   **Business Model:** Their model is centered on **driving digital revenue through audience data**. By delivering engaging content to grow their audience, they gather comprehensive data on listener behaviors and demographics. They monetize this by offering highly visible, data-backed advertising opportunities to their clients.

### 3. Radio Mitre SA
*   **Key Contact:** Hernan Vilaplana, Head of Digital Business and CFO
*   **What They Do:** Radio broadcasting, streaming audio, and podcasting.
*   **Business Model:** They monetize and scale their growing audience through **programmatic advertising**. By managing streaming audio and podcast ads programmatically, they simplify the ad sales process, meet high audience demand, and maximize overall ad revenue.

### 4. NOVA Entertainment
*   **Key Contact:** Tim Armstrong, Director of Digital Capability and Data
*   **What They Do:** An independent audio entertainment destination (radio broadcasting).
*   **Business Model:** They create and deliver audio entertainment experiences to listeners. They monetize through **audio advertising campaigns**, utilizing technology to streamline campaign management. This drives operational efficiencies, allowing them to deliver consistent, high-quality ad experiences to their clients.

### 5. Organización Radial Olímpica
*   **Key Contact:** Rafael Páez Rodríguez, National Production Manager
*   **What They Do:** Radio broadcasting and audio streaming services.
*   **Business Model:** They utilize **audience data for dual monetization**. They use listener data to guide internal programming decisions (keeping audiences engaged) while simultaneously improving "inventory monetization" (ad space). They also act as a partner to advertisers, helping clients optimize their audio campaigns based on this data.

### 6. ARN (Australian Radio Network)
*   **Key Contact:** Jeremy Webb, Ad Operations and Programmatic Director
*   **What They Do:** Radio broadcasting and digital audio platform.
*   **Business Model:** As an audio platform, they rely on **programmatic ad operations** to monetize their streams. Their business model depends on having a fast, intuitive, and user-focused platform to efficiently manage, sell, and deliver high volumes of digital audio ad campaigns.

### 7. AdLarge (representing the cabana podcast portfolio)
*   **Key Contact:** Cathy Csukas, CEO and Founder
*   **What They Do:** Podcast advertising representation and network management.
*   **Business Model:** They operate as an **advertising sales and representation network** for podcasts. Their model relies on independent measurement and comparative data (like Triton's Podcast Metrics Report) to prove the strength, reach, and ROI of their podcast portfolios to potential advertisers.

### 8. NRM Comunicaciones
*   **Key Contact:** Ayme Ferrer
*   **What They Do:** Media and communications (advertising campaign management).
*   **Business Model:** They manage and sell **advertising campaigns (flights)** across their media properties. Their business model relies on centralized, easy-to-use tools to quickly set up, copy, and manage ad campaigns for their clients, reducing operational friction in the media sales process. 

---
**Summary of the Common Business Model:**
Across all these companies, the traditional "sell radio spots" model has evolved into a **Digital Audio & Data Monetization** model. They use technology to capture first-party listener data, package their streaming and podcast inventory for programmatic (automated) ad sales, and use independent metrics to prove ROI to advertisers.

# Citadel & Citadel Securities — Executive Summary

## Overview

Citadel is a unique financial institution that operates as both a **multi-strategy hedge fund** (Citadel LLC) and one of the world's largest **market makers** (Citadel Securities). Founded by Kenneth C. Griffin in 1990 from his Harvard dorm room with $265,000 in seed capital, Citadel has grown to become the most profitable hedge fund in history, with cumulative net gains of **$83 billion** since inception. Citadel Securities, founded in 2002, executes over **20% of all US equity trades** and generated **$12.2 billion in trading revenue** in 2025.

## Core Philosophy

Citadel's philosophy is built on three pillars:

1. **Pod Shop Model**: Semi-autonomous teams targeting 2–3% alpha, leveraged 5x
2. **Risk Management First**: Proprietary systems monitor correlation across dozens of teams
3. **Scale Discipline**: Return capital rather than grow beyond optimal size

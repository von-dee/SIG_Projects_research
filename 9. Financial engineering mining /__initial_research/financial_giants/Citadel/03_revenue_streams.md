# Citadel — Revenue Streams

Citadel operates two distinct revenue models through its dual structure:
1. **Citadel LLC (Hedge Fund)**: Management fees + performance fees
2. **Citadel Securities (Market Maker)**: Trading profits + spreads

This dual structure creates diversified revenue streams that are largely uncorrelated, providing stability and growth potential.

# Renaissance Technologies — Business Model

## Overview

Renaissance Technologies operates a **quantitative research-driven investment management** business model. Unlike traditional asset managers who rely on fundamental analysis and human judgment, RenTech's model is built entirely on mathematical models, algorithms, and computational power. The firm treats financial markets as complex systems that can be understood and predicted through advanced statistical methods.

## Unique Business Model Characteristics

### 1. Employee-Only Medallion Fund
The Medallion Fund is unique in the industry:
- **Closed to outside investors since 1993**
- **Employees are the sole beneficiaries**
- **Creates perfect incentive alignment**
- **Allows for higher-risk, higher-return strategies**
- **Eliminates redemption pressure**

### 2. Scientific Recruitment Model
- **No finance majors**: Almost exclusively hires PhDs in physics, math, CS
- **No Wall Street background**: Prefers academic and research backgrounds
- **Puzzle-solving culture**: Recruitment through mathematical competitions
- **Long-term retention**: Extremely low turnover due to compensation and culture

### 3. Black Box Operations
- **Extreme secrecy**: No public disclosures of strategies
- **Compartmentalization**: Research teams work independently
- **No external audits of strategies**: Only financial audits
- **Proprietary everything**: Custom data, custom models, custom infrastructure

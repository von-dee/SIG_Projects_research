# Two Sigma — Business Model

## Overview

Two Sigma operates a **technology-driven asset management** business model. The firm combines quantitative research, alternative data, and machine learning to generate alpha for institutional investors. Unlike market makers, Two Sigma does not trade for its own account (primarily); instead, it manages client assets through a variety of fund vehicles.

## Risks to the Business Model

1. **Governance Risk**: Founder split, internal politics
2. **Compliance Risk**: $165M refund for rogue researcher scandal
3. **Crowding**: Popular quant factors becoming commoditized
4. **Model Decay**: Alpha generation degrading over time
5. **Fee Pressure**: Institutional investors demanding lower fees
6. **Talent Competition**: Tech firms offering higher compensation
